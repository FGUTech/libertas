## TL;DR

This week revealed a coordinated global assault on digital freedom: age verification laws expanding across platforms and governments threaten to end anonymous internet use, while 30 governments deployed transnational repression and systematic financial censorship by payment platforms documented new forms of control. Freedom tech organizations pushed back with enhanced censorship measurement tools and legal challenges.

## Top Signals

### Age Verification as Universal ID System

- [Age Verification Expanding Across Platforms, OS, and Government Digital ID Systems](/posts/age-verification-expanding-across-platforms-os-and-governmen) — Rapid expansion from platforms to operating systems and national digital ID frameworks threatens online anonymity
- [GUARD Act Would Mandate ID Checks, End Anonymous Internet Use](/posts/guard-act-would-mandate-id-checks-end-anonymous-internet-use) — Framed as AI safety for kids, would require age verification for all AI-powered tools

The convergence of age verification mandates across platforms, operating systems, and government systems represents the most serious threat to anonymous internet use since its creation. What began as "protecting children" is becoming universal identity verification infrastructure.
### Government Surveillance Powers

- [30 Governments Used Transnational Repression in 2025, Freedom House Reports](/posts/30-governments-used-transnational-repression-in-2025-freedom) — 126 new incidents targeting critics abroad through detention and violence
- [Canadian Civil Society Coalition Calls for Full Withdrawal of Surveillance Bill C-22](/posts/canadian-civil-society-coalition-calls-for-full-withdrawal-o) — Bill would mandate metadata retention and surveillance backdoors
- [EFF Opposes Insufficient FISA 702 Reauthorization Bill Lacking Warrant Requirements](/posts/eff-opposes-insufficient-fisa-702-reauthorization-bill-lacki) — Fails to require warrants for FBI searches of Americans' communications

### Corporate-Government Data Sharing

- [Palantir's $130M IRS Contract Enables Mass Financial Surveillance Including Crypto Tracking](/posts/palantir-s-130m-irs-contract-enables-mass-financial-surveill) — Aggregates federal databases and tracks crypto transactions
- [Citizen Lab Exposes How Ad-Tech Data Enables Government Location Surveillance](/posts/citizen-lab-exposes-how-ad-tech-data-enables-government-loca) — ICE using ad-tech data to circumvent legal protections
- [EFF Exposes ICE Data Access After Google Broke Privacy Promise to Client](/posts/eff-exposes-ice-data-access-after-google-broke-privacy-promi) — Corporate failure to protect users from government overreach
### Payment Platform Weaponization

- [New Book Documents Financial Censorship by Payment Platforms](/posts/new-book-documents-financial-censorship-by-payment-platforms) — Systematic blocking by PayPal, Venmo based on speech

### Legal Weaponization

- [Nigerian Cybercrime Law Used to Target 24+ Journalists and Activists](/posts/nigerian-cybercrime-law-used-to-target-24-journalists-and-ac) — 'Cyberstalking' provisions criminalize critical speech
- [Press Freedom Groups Call for Repeal of Turkey's Disinformation Law Used Against Journalists](/posts/press-freedom-groups-call-for-repeal-of-turkey-s-disinformat) — Vague definitions enable journalist prosecution

Financial censorship has emerged as a primary control mechanism, with payment platforms systematically blocking accounts based on speech while governments weaponize cybercrime laws against critics.
### Censorship Measurement Advances

- [OONI Releases 2025 Year in Review: Major Platform Updates and Censorship Measurement Advances](/posts/ooni-releases-2025-year-in-review-major-platform-updates-and) — Multiplatform mobile apps and anonymous credential systems
- [Bellingcat Guide: Using China's Xiaohongshu for Open-Source Research Under Digital Oppression](/posts/bellingcat-guide-using-china-s-xiaohongshu-for-open-source-r) — Circumventing AI surveillance and real-time censorship

### Legal Defense & Platform Protection

- [EFF Publishes DMCA Compliance Guide for Decentralized Social Platform Operators](/posts/eff-publishes-dmca-compliance-guide-for-decentralized-social) — Protecting Mastodon, Bluesky operators from liability
- [Reddit CLO: Section 230 Protects Decentralized Community Moderation, Not Just Big Tech](/posts/reddit-clo-section-230-protects-decentralized-community-mode) — Defending volunteer moderators and community governance

Freedom tech organizations responded with enhanced measurement capabilities and legal frameworks to protect decentralized platforms from regulatory capture.

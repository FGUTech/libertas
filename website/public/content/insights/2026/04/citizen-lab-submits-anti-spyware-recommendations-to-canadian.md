# Citizen Lab Submits Anti-Spyware Recommendations to Canadian Parliament Committee

**TL;DR:** Citizen Lab recommended comprehensive spyware regulations to Canada's intelligence oversight committee, arguing government spyware use creates human rights and national security risks.

## Key Points

- Citizen Lab submitted anti-spyware recommendations to National Security and Intelligence Committee of Parliamentarians (NSICOP) in June 2023
- Submission argues Canadian government spyware use introduces human rights and national security risks
- Recommends comprehensive regulations and oversight mechanisms for mercenary spyware
- NSICOP report and submissions were published publicly earlier in 2024
- Highlights growing policy debate over state use of commercial surveillance tools
- Represents push for legislative guardrails on government surveillance capabilities
- Part of broader international movement to regulate mercenary spyware market

# Hack-for-Hire Phishing Campaign Targets MENA Civil Society

**TL;DR:** Access Now reports a sophisticated phishing campaign targeting civil society activists across the MENA region, part of broader espionage efforts against human rights organizations.

## Key Points

- Access Now documented hack-for-hire phishing campaign in MENA region
- Campaign specifically targets civil society organizations and activists
- Attacks described as 'espionage for repression' indicating state-level threat actors
- Part of broader pattern of digital surveillance against human rights defenders
- Access Now Helpline Team involved in documenting and responding to incidents
- Highlights ongoing digital security threats facing freedom tech users in authoritarian contexts

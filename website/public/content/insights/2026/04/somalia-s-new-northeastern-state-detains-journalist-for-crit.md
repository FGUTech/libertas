# Somalia's New Northeastern State Detains Journalist for Criticizing President

**TL;DR:** Journalist Abdiqani Abdirahman Mohamed detained without charge since March 26 by Northeastern State authorities over video criticizing President Abdiqadir Ahmed Aw-Ali.

## Key Points

- Armed police arrested Abdiqani Abdirahman Mohamed (Dhulmar Media founder) in Las-Anod on March 26
- Arrest followed publication of video interview criticizing Northeastern State President Abdiqadir Ahmed Aw-Ali
- Interview featured former presidential adviser accusing president of being ineffective and failing to pay security forces
- Police also arrested interviewee Abdirisak Mohamed Warsame on suspicion of spreading fake news and libel
- Court remanded journalist for seven days pending investigation as of March 27
- Committee to Protect Journalists calls detention a worrying sign of intolerance to criticism
- Northeastern State declared Somalia's sixth federal member state in January 2026, with disputed territorial control

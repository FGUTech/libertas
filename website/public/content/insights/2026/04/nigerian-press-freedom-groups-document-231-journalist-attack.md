# Nigerian Press Freedom Groups Document 231+ Journalist Attacks Under Tinubu Administration

**TL;DR:** Press freedom organizations formally challenge VP Shettima's claim of no journalist harassment in Nigeria, documenting 231 verified attacks since 2023 including detentions, assaults, and killings.

## Key Points

- Joint letter from 6 major press freedom organizations to President Tinubu disputes VP's claims
- Centre for Journalism Innovation documented 231 verified attacks on press since Tinubu took office in 2023
- At least 56 journalists assaulted during #EndBadGovernance protests, 18 had bullets/tear gas fired at them
- Onifade Emmanuel Pelumi killed in police custody 2020, body still not returned to family after 5 years
- Pattern of impunity: CPJ documented 23 journalist killings in Nigeria since 1992, 13 work-related
- Organizations warn of expected spike in attacks during 2027 election period
- Government sought to appeal court judgment ordering improved press freedom protections
- Signatories include CPJ, Media Rights Agenda, International Press Centre, and other established groups

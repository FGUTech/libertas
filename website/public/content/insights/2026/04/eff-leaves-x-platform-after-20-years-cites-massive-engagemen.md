# EFF Leaves X Platform After 20 Years, Cites Massive Engagement Drop and Policy Failures

**TL;DR:** Electronic Frontier Foundation exits X after engagement plummeted 97% since 2018, while staying on other platforms to reach vulnerable communities needing digital rights advocacy.

## Key Points

- EFF logging off X after nearly 20 years due to dramatic engagement decline
- X posts now receive less than 3% of views compared to 2018 Twitter posts
- Monthly impressions dropped from 50-100 million (2018) to 2 million (2024)
- Musk fired entire human rights team and staff fighting censorship in repressive regimes
- EFF demanded transparent moderation, E2E encryption for DMs, and user control improvements
- Staying on Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube to reach marginalized communities
- Platform choice reflects strategic resource allocation for maximum digital rights impact
- Moving focus to Bluesky, Mastodon, LinkedIn and other platforms

# Alabama City Prosecutes Protester for Penis Costume at Anti-Trump Rally

**TL;DR:** Fairhope, Alabama arrested and is prosecuting a 62-year-old protester for wearing an inflatable penis costume at a political rally, doubling down with additional charges despite viral backlash.

## Key Points

- Renea Gamble, 62, arrested at October 2025 No Kings protest in Fairhope, Alabama for wearing inflatable penis costume
- Police Cpl. Andrew Babb threw Gamble to ground after she attempted to leave, claiming costume violated 'community standards'
- Body camera footage contradicts officer's radio report of events, shows Gamble asking if detained before trying to leave
- City added disturbing peace and false name charges after initial disorderly conduct/resisting arrest
- Case part of broader censorship trend - Fairhope library lost state funding for refusing to move LGBTQ+ books
- Trial set for April 15 after multiple delays, despite expectations case would be dropped
- Mayor and city council publicly endorsed arrest, calling costume 'not acceptable' and violating 'community standards'

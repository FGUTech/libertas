# Iranian-American Women Officials Condemn Trump's War Threats Against Iran

**TL;DR:** Iranian-American women in elected office reject Trump's threats against Iran, arguing war undermines the 'Woman, Life, Freedom' movement and Iranian self-determination.

## Key Points

- Group of Iranian-American women officials released letter opposing U.S.-Israeli war on Iran
- Led by Rep. Yassamin Ansari (D-AZ), first Iranian-American Democrat in Congress
- Letter rejects 'false choice between repression at home and devastation from abroad'
- Trump threatened to kill 'whole civilization' if Iran doesn't reopen Strait of Hormuz
- References 'Woman, Life, Freedom' protests sparked by Mahsa Amini's killing in 2022
- Ansari plans impeachment articles against Defense Secretary Pete Hegseth for war crimes
- Iranian diaspora reportedly divided but polling shows growing opposition to war
- Signers argue war encourages further government crackdowns against Iranian protesters

# Russia Labels Press Freedom Org IWMF as 'Undesirable Organization'

**TL;DR:** Russia designated the International Women's Media Foundation as an 'undesirable organization,' banning its operations and criminalizing support with up to 6 years prison.

## Key Points

- Russia labeled International Women's Media Foundation (IWMF) as 'undesirable organization' in January 2026
- Designation bans IWMF from operating in Russia and criminalizes participation with up to 6 years prison
- Distributing IWMF content or donating to the organization now carries administrative and criminal penalties
- IWMF was not informed of reasons behind the classification [Source]
- Committee to Protect Journalists and Journalist Assistance Network condemned the move
- JAN includes CPJ, IWMF, Freedom of the Press Foundation, PEN America, and Reporters Committee for Freedom of the Press
- Tactic aims to sever support networks that sustain independent journalists under threat
- Pattern matches previous Russian use of 'undesirable' classification against press freedom organizations

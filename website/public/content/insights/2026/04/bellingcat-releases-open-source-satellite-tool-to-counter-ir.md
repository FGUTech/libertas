# Bellingcat Releases Open-Source Satellite Tool to Counter Iran Internet Blackout

**TL;DR:** Bellingcat launched a free damage assessment tool using ESA satellite data to monitor Iran conflict destruction, bypassing commercial imagery restrictions and internet blackouts that limit information flow.

## Key Points

- Iran Conflict Damage Proxy Map uses free Sentinel-1 SAR satellite data to detect building damage
- Tool addresses commercial satellite imagery restrictions and Iranian internet blackouts limiting conflict visibility
- Pixel-Wise T-Test algorithm compares current imagery to pre-war baselines with 87% accuracy (AUC=0.87)
- Validated against 2+ million UN-labeled building footprints across Gaza, Ukraine, Sudan, Syria, Iraq
- Successfully identified damage at Tehran's Valiasr Barracks, Isfahan IRGC compound, and Fath Air Base
- Avoids AI overfitting problems by comparing pixels only to their own historical patterns
- Democratizes conflict monitoring when governments control information infrastructure
- Extends beyond Iran to track regional targets hit by Iranian drones and missiles

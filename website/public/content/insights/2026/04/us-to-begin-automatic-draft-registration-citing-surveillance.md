# US to Begin Automatic Draft Registration, Citing Surveillance Goals

**TL;DR:** The Selective Service System will automatically register draft-eligible men starting late 2026, abandoning self-registration in favor of federal database integration for 'surveillance' purposes.

## Key Points

- Selective Service System switching to automatic registration by December 2026 after decades of self-registration
- Change driven by declining registration rates (81% in 2024, down 3% from prior year) and potential China conflict
- System will integrate with Social Security Administration and Census Bureau databases
- Government official explicitly cited 'surveillance' as a goal alongside 'manpower generation and management'
- Trump administration described as having 'obsession' with creating 'comprehensive federal databases'
- Failure to register remains felony with 5-year prison sentence, bars federal jobs and student loans
- Represents expansion of government surveillance infrastructure using military conscription authority

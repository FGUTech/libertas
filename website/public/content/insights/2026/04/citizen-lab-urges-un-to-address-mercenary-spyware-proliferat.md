# Citizen Lab Urges UN to Address Mercenary Spyware Proliferation

**TL;DR:** Citizen Lab submitted UN recommendations calling for states to stop outsourcing digital surveillance operations, warning that mercenary spyware threatens democracy and human rights.

## Key Points

- Citizen Lab submitted formal recommendations to UN Working Group on the Use of Mercenaries
- Calls for states to end reliance on private sector for digital surveillance operations
- Warns that lack of accountability mechanisms enables mercenary spyware proliferation
- Identifies privatization of state security as key factor in surveillance expansion
- Highlights risks of democratic backsliding and human rights abuses
- Points to 'entrenchment of impunity' as systematic problem
- Represents formal international advocacy against commercial surveillance industry

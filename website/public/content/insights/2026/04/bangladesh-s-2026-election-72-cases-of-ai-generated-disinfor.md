# Bangladesh's 2026 Election: 72 Cases of AI-Generated Disinformation Documented

**TL;DR:** Study identifies 72 cases of AI-manipulated content during Bangladesh's February 2026 election, including deepfakes of political figures and fabricated media photocards designed to shape electoral outcomes.

## Key Points

- First comprehensive study of AI disinformation in Bangladesh's post-uprising election identified 72 manipulation cases
- 49% of AI content created entirely fictional realities about political leaders' activities and statements
- 28% involved fake statement attributions using AI-edited photocards mimicking trusted media outlets
- Deepfakes targeted all major parties: fabricated videos of exiled PM Sheikh Hasina and fake endorsement from Malaysian PM Mahathir
- AI-generated images showed new PM Tarique Rahman at events he never attended
- Synthetic content exploited shooting of activist Osman Hadi, spreading false recovery images
- Fabricated photocards from established outlets like Somoy TV and Channel i amplified false quotes
- Study demonstrates systematic weaponization of AI across multiple political factions

# AI Tool Grok Amplifies Online Violence Against Nigerian Women

**TL;DR:** X's AI assistant Grok is being weaponized to generate non-consensual sexualized images, systematizing online abuse that already disproportionately targets Nigerian women with weak regulatory oversight.

## Key Points

- Grok AI tool integrated into X platform being used to create non-consensual sexualized images of women and minors
- 58% of online abuse cases in Nigeria target women, with X being primary platform (34% of cases) [Gatefield 2024 report]
- Only 24% of Nigerians find X responsive to complaints about online harm
- 45% of Nigerian women have experienced cyberstalking according to ActionAid Nigeria
- Gatefield projects 70 million Nigerian women could face AI-facilitated abuse annually by 2030
- X's monetization system allows perpetrators to earn money from viral abusive content
- Nigeria's fragmented AI regulatory environment creates enforcement gaps across multiple agencies
- Tool transforms existing human-driven harassment into systematized, scalable abuse

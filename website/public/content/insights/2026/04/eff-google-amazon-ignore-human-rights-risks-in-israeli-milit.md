# EFF: Google & Amazon Ignore Human Rights Risks in Israeli Military AI Contract

**TL;DR:** EFF reports Google and Amazon continue Project Nimbus cloud/AI services to Israeli military despite internal warnings and documented human rights risks, while refusing transparency or meaningful action.

## Key Points

- Google's internal assessments warned of human rights abuse risks before Project Nimbus contract signing
- Contract provides Israeli Ministry of Defense with cloud computing, AI services, data analysis tools
- Amazon has completely ignored EFF letters; Google has promised responses for 1.5+ years without action
- Microsoft precedent: required public leak to investigate and confirm Israeli government service misuse
- Google's public claims about contract limitations contradict internal documentation and reporting
- Both companies' AI principles commit to human rights oversight and risk mitigation
- EFF argues companies have knowledge and leverage to act but are choosing willful blindness
- Pattern shows major cloud providers enabling surveillance infrastructure while avoiding accountability

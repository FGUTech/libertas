# EFF's Cindy Cohn Promotes Privacy Book at DC Events

**TL;DR:** EFF Executive Director Cindy Cohn is hosting two DC events to discuss her new book about 30 years of fighting digital surveillance, with proceeds benefiting EFF.

## Key Points

- EFF Executive Director Cindy Cohn promoting book 'Privacy's Defender: My Thirty-Year Fight Against Digital Surveillance'
- Two Washington DC events scheduled for April 13-14, 2026
- April 13 event features conversation with AAPB Executive Director Gigi Sohn at Busboys & Poets
- April 14 event hosted with Women in Security and Privacy (WISP) featuring American University's Chelsea Horne
- Book endorsed by Edward Snowden as 'compelling account' and 'inspiring call to action'
- All book proceeds benefit EFF's digital rights advocacy work
- Events focus on data security, federal data access, and digital rights

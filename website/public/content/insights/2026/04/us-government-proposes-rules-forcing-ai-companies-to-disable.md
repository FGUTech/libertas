# US Government Proposes Rules Forcing AI Companies to Disable Safety Features

**TL;DR:** General Services Administration proposes procurement rules requiring contractors to license AI systems for 'all lawful purposes' and disable safety guardrails that might refuse government requests.

## Key Points

- GSA proposes new federal procurement rules targeting AI contractors
- Rules would require companies to license AI systems for 'all lawful purposes' to government
- Companies must disable safety guardrails that might refuse government data requests
- Follows ongoing dispute between Department of Defense and Anthropic over surveillance use
- EFF, CDT, Protect Democracy Project, and EPIC filed joint opposition comments
- Rules would become standard components of every federal contract if adopted
- Government using procurement power to bypass companies' refusal to enable mass surveillance
- Proposal includes 'technologically incoherent anti-Woke requirements' [Source]

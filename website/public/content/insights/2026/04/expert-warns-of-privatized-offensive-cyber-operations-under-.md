# Expert Warns of Privatized Offensive Cyber Operations Under Trump Admin

**TL;DR:** Citizen Lab director warns Trump administration may authorize private firms to conduct offensive cyber operations, creating unprecedented risks for oversight, civil liberties, and global cyber security.

## Key Points

- Citizen Lab director Ron Deibert published warning in Lawfare about potential policy shift
- Trump administration may authorize private companies to conduct offensive cyber operations (OCOs)
- OCOs aim to disrupt and destroy adversary systems, unlike espionage which gathers information
- Would represent unprecedented departure from U.S. military convention and international norms
- Risks include compromised oversight, counterintelligence vulnerabilities, and empowering corrupt industry
- Could accelerate global cyber arms race and increase risks for civilians and states
- Deibert recommends international coalition against these developments
- Calls for increased resources for civil society watchdog organizations

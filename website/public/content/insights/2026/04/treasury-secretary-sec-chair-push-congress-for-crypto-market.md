# Treasury Secretary, SEC Chair Push Congress for Crypto Market Structure Bill

**TL;DR:** Treasury Secretary Bessent, SEC Chair Atkins, and former Crypto Czar Sacks urge immediate Senate passage of the Clarity Act to establish clear regulatory boundaries for crypto and prevent innovation flight overseas.

## Key Points

- Treasury Secretary Scott Bessent calls for Senate Banking Committee to advance Clarity Act to Trump's desk
- SEC Chair Paul Atkins and former White House Crypto Czar David Sacks endorse immediate action
- Clarity Act would establish regulatory boundaries between SEC and CFTC for digital assets
- Bill defines token security status, trading platform pathways, and anti-fraud measures
- Companion legislation to Genius Act (stablecoin framework) signed by Trump last year
- Bessent warns crypto innovation moving to Abu Dhabi and Singapore due to regulatory uncertainty
- Supporters argue bill would keep blockchain innovation and tax revenue in U.S.

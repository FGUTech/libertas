# Rwanda Reaffirms Crypto Ban After Bybit Adds Rwandan Franc Support

**TL;DR:** Rwanda's central bank swiftly reaffirmed its cryptocurrency ban after Bybit enabled Rwandan franc trading on its P2P platform, highlighting ongoing state resistance to monetary sovereignty bypass attempts.

## Key Points

- Central Bank of Rwanda restated crypto prohibition after Bybit added Rwandan franc to P2P marketplace
- Bank warned against crypto-franc conversions, citing financial risks and lack of legal protection
- Bybit did not indicate whether it secured regulatory approval before enabling the feature
- Rwanda has maintained restrictive crypto stance since 2018 to protect financial stability
- Country is developing state-controlled e-franc digital currency in proof-of-concept phase
- Draft framework from March would license crypto service providers with strict limitations
- Chainalysis data shows Rwanda ranks among lower crypto adoption markets in region

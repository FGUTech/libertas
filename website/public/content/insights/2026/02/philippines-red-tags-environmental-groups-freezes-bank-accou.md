# Philippines 'Red-Tags' Environmental Groups, Freezes Bank Accounts of Solar/Disaster Relief NGOs

**TL;DR:** Philippine government systematically labels environmental defenders as 'terrorists,' freezing bank accounts of NGOs providing solar power and disaster relief to rural communities.

## Key Points

- Government task forces accuse environmental NGOs of terrorism/communist links through 'red-tagging' practice
- Leyte Center for Development (LCDe) had foreign donor accounts frozen, director faces terrorism financing charges
- Sibat nonprofit installing solar-powered water pumps also red-tagged and harassed by military
- UN experts confirm systematic targeting of environmental defenders, Indigenous peoples, and humanitarian workers
- Financial censorship prevents crucial disaster relief as typhoon-prone regions lose emergency services
- Organizations must bring EU diplomats to meetings to demonstrate legitimacy to local officials
- Practice intensified under Duterte (2016-2022), continues under current President Marcos Jr

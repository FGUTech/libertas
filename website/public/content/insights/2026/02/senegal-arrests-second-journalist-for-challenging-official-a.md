# Senegal Arrests Second Journalist for Challenging Official Account of Student Death

**TL;DR:** Senegalese authorities arrested freelance commentator Abdou Nguer for questioning prosecutor's denial of torture in student death during university protest crackdown, marking escalating press freedom concerns.

## Key Points

- Abdou Nguer arrested February 19, 2026 for challenging prosecutor's statement on student death cause
- Charged with spreading false news for disputing official dismissal of torture allegations
- Second journalist detained over university protest coverage after Sen TV's Modou Fall (arrested Feb 11)
- Nguer questioned prosecutor Ousmane Ndoye's claim student fell from building vs medical autopsy showing traumas
- Student died after police stormed university campus in Dakar during protests
- Nguer previously detained 7 months in 2025, served 3 months for 'offending head of state'
- Pattern of false news charges targeting journalists covering state violence and public affairs
- Committee to Protect Journalists calls systematic intimidation of Senegalese press

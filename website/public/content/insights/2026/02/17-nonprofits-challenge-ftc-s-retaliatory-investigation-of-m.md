# 17 Nonprofits Challenge FTC's Retaliatory Investigation of Media Matters

**TL;DR:** Coalition led by The Intercept's Press Freedom Defense Fund filed amicus brief to stop FTC's allegedly retaliatory investigation of Media Matters, citing pattern of government intimidation tactics.

## Key Points

- 17 nonprofits filed amicus brief in U.S. Court of Appeals for D.C. Circuit
- Challenging FTC investigation into Media Matters for America over Trump administration criticism
- Brief argues investigation is retaliatory and part of broader government intimidation pattern
- Coalition includes EFF, Freedom of the Press Foundation, and other freedom tech organizations
- Seeks to uphold preliminary injunction against the investigation
- Brief cites similar tactics across Justice Department and FCC under current administration
- Legal strategy aims to prevent chilling effect on nonprofit journalism and research

# Cameroon Police Seize Journalist Equipment, Target Sources in Deportation Investigation

**TL;DR:** Cameroonian police detained 4 journalists covering secret US migrant deportations, seized their devices, and threatened to access confidential sources by circumventing passwords.

## Key Points

- Four journalists detained 5 hours by Cameroon judicial police while covering secret US deportations
- Police seized phones, laptop, and other equipment from AP reporters and freelancer
- Authorities investigating for criminal trespass, equipment not yet returned
- Police reportedly plan to circumvent device passwords to access private information
- Lawyer concerned anonymous sources could be compromised through device forensics
- Journalists threatened with being 'crushed' and told one would 'disappear without a trace'
- Incident highlights operational security risks for journalists in hostile environments
- Demonstrates how device seizures can expose source networks and communications

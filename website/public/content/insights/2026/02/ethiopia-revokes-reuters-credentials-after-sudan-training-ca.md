# Ethiopia Revokes Reuters Credentials After Sudan Training Camp Investigation

**TL;DR:** Ethiopia revoked Reuters journalists' accreditation following report alleging secret UAE-financed camp training Sudan's RSF fighters, part of broader media crackdown ahead of June elections.

## Key Points

- Ethiopian Media Authority revoked accreditation of three Reuters journalists on February 14, 2026
- Action followed Reuters February 10 investigation alleging Ethiopia hosts UAE-financed camp training Sudan's RSF fighters
- Reuters barred from covering African Union summit in Addis Ababa
- Part of escalating press restrictions including suspension of Deutsche Welle correspondents in October
- Wazema Radio's license revoked in January for reporting against 'national interest'
- Crackdown intensifying ahead of June 2026 general election amid ongoing regional conflicts
- Pattern demonstrates government using regulatory power to silence critical reporting on sensitive geopolitical activities

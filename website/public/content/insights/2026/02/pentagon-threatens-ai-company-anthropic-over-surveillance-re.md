# Pentagon Threatens AI Company Anthropic Over Surveillance Restrictions

**TL;DR:** Department of Defense threatening to label Anthropic a "supply chain risk" after company refused to lift restrictions on surveillance and autonomous weapons use of their AI.

## Key Points

- Pentagon gave ultimatum to Anthropic demanding removal of restrictions on military AI use
- Anthropic maintains "bright red lines" against surveillance of US persons and autonomous weapons
- DoD threatens "supply chain risk" label that would ban other defense contractors from using Anthropic's AI
- Dispute began after Anthropic suspected their AI was used in January 3, 2026 attack on Venezuela via Palantir partnership
- Anthropic was first AI company cleared for classified operations in 2025
- EFF urges Anthropic to resist government pressure and maintain ethical restrictions
- Case represents broader pattern of government pressure on tech companies to enable surveillance

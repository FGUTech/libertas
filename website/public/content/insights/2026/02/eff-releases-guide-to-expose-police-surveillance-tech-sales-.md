# EFF Releases Guide to Expose Police Surveillance Tech Sales Hype

**TL;DR:** EFF, CJJ, and IPVM released 'Selling Safety' report helping journalists critically examine police surveillance technology claims and expose industry tactics that exploit taxpayers while eroding civil liberties.

## Key Points

- EFF, Center for Just Journalism, and IPVM released joint 'Selling Safety' report for journalists
- Guide helps cut through surveillance tech marketing claims that are 'often misleading'
- Police tech industry described as 'unregulated, unexamined, and consequential' with billion-dollar profit motives
- Companies manufacture appearance of effectiveness rather than measuring actual results
- Report provides tools for better questions, understanding incentives, and finding accountability stories
- Surveillance technologies spreading faster than public understanding or oversight
- EFF maintains Street-Level Surveillance hub and Atlas of Surveillance mapping tools
- Resource aims to protect taxpayer dollars and civil liberties through critical journalism

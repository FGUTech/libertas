# Signal Announces New Features Roll Call Blog Series for App Improvements

**TL;DR:** Signal launched a periodic blog series to highlight new features and quality of life improvements, emphasizing their commitment to truly private messaging without data harvesting for ads or AI training.

## Key Points

- Signal announced new 'New Features Roll Call' periodic blog series
- Series will highlight multiple features and improvements in single posts
- Signal reaffirms commitment to private messaging without data harvesting
- Emphasizes protection against targeted advertising and AI training on user data
- Focus on improving user experience while maintaining privacy guarantees
- Represents ongoing effort to communicate development progress to users
- Reinforces Signal's position as privacy-first alternative to mainstream platforms

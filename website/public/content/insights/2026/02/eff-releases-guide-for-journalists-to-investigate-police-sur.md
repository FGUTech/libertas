# EFF Releases Guide for Journalists to Investigate Police Surveillance Tech

**TL;DR:** EFF, Center for Just Journalism, and IPVM released 'Selling Safety' report to help journalists cut through police tech marketing hype and report accurately on surveillance tools' costs, effectiveness, and privacy impacts.

## Key Points

- New 'Selling Safety' report published by EFF, Center for Just Journalism, and IPVM
- Guide helps journalists investigate police surveillance technology claims and marketing
- Report exposes how police tech companies manufacture appearance of effectiveness without measurement
- Industry described as 'unregulated, unexamined, and consequential' with profit motive driving adoption
- Provides tools for journalists to ask better questions about costs, privacy, and accountability
- Addresses pattern of unsubstantiated claims from surveillance technology vendors
- Complements EFF's existing Street-Level Surveillance hub and Atlas of Surveillance resources

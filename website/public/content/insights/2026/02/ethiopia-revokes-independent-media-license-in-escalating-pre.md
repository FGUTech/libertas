# Ethiopia Revokes Independent Media License in Escalating Press Crackdown

**TL;DR:** Ethiopian authorities revoked Addis Standard's media license citing vague 'national interest' violations, part of broader crackdown including Reuters accreditation denials and newsroom raids.

## Key Points

- Ethiopian Media Authority (EMA) revoked Addis Standard's online media registration on February 24, 2026
- EMA cited 'media ethics violations' and threats to 'national interests' without providing specific evidence
- Editor-in-Chief Yonas Kedir denies receiving formal violation notices, calls claims 'factually incorrect'
- Part of broader media suppression: Reuters journalists denied accreditation, AFP reporter blocked from Tigray travel
- Addis Standard previously raided in April 2025 with equipment seized and managers detained
- Creates immediate need for censorship-resistant publishing and secure communication channels
- Pattern indicates systematic dismantling of independent journalism infrastructure in Ethiopia

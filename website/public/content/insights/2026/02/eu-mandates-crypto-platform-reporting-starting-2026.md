# EU Mandates Crypto Platform Reporting Starting 2026

**TL;DR:** EU implementing new crypto tax rules requiring platforms to report user data and transactions to authorities, significantly reducing financial privacy for digital assets starting 2026.

## Key Points

- EU introducing mandatory crypto transaction reporting requirements for platforms
- New rules take effect in 2026, targeting tax transparency for digital assets
- Platforms will be required to report user data and transaction details to authorities
- Represents major shift toward surveillance-based approach to crypto taxation
- Affects all cryptocurrency users operating through regulated platforms in EU
- Part of broader global trend toward crypto transaction monitoring
- Could influence platform compliance costs and user privacy practices

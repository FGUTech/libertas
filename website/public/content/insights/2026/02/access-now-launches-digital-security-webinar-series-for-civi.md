# Access Now Launches Digital Security Webinar Series for Civil Society

**TL;DR:** Access Now's Digital Security Helpline launches 'Safe and Strong' webinar series targeting spear-phishing threats against activists, journalists, and civil society with expert guidance from Citizen Lab and security researchers.

## Key Points

- Access Now launching 'Safe and Strong' webinar series on digital security for civil society
- First session March 10, 2026 focuses on spear-phishing attacks targeting activists and journalists
- Expert speakers include Citizen Lab's Rebekah Brown, Lookout's Justin Albrecht, and regional digital security specialists
- Content draws from documented attacks in Eastern Europe and Middle East regions
- Initiative aims to share actionable insights as digital attacks against civil society grow more complex
- Webinar targets high-profile activists, journalists, and NGO members who face sophisticated social engineering
- Event brings together civil society, private sector, and support organizations for resilience building

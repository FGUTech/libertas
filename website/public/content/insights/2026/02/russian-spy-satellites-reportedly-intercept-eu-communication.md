# Russian Spy Satellites Reportedly Intercept EU Communications Infrastructure

**TL;DR:** European officials believe Russian space vehicles have intercepted communications from dozens of EU satellites, potentially compromising sensitive data and enabling trajectory manipulation.

## Key Points

- Two Russian space vehicles reportedly intercepted communications from at least 12 European satellites
- Officials claim interceptions risk compromising sensitive transmitted information
- Moscow could potentially manipulate satellite trajectories or crash intercepted satellites
- Russian shadowing of European satellites intensified over past three years
- Activity coincides with heightened tensions following Ukraine invasion
- Demonstrates vulnerabilities in satellite-based communications infrastructure
- Highlights need for improved encryption and anti-jamming in space-based comms

# Signal Announces New President Role to Focus on Sustainability and Strategy

**TL;DR:** Signal appointed a new President on September 12 to focus on long-term sustainability, strategy, and communications for the encrypted messaging platform.

## Key Points

- Signal created new President position filled by longtime board member
- Role focuses on financial sustainability and strategic planning
- President will work on broadening Signal's public communications
- Appointee has been involved with Signal since RedPhone/TextSecure era
- Signal emphasizes importance of building 'long taproot' for future resilience
- Organizational restructuring aimed at strengthening both app and organization
- Move signals Signal's focus on long-term institutional stability

# Journalist Safety Expert Highlights Digital Security Tools for High-Risk Environments

**TL;DR:** Free Press Unlimited's Pablo Arcuri outlines digital security priorities for journalists, emphasizing phone protection and tools like VPNs while connecting online disinformation to physical violence.

## Key Points

- Pablo Arcuri from Free Press Unlimited interviewed on multi-dimensional threats to journalists
- Primary digital advice: treat phones as most sensitive equipment, avoid suspicious apps/links
- State-sponsored spyware like Pegasus still often requires user interaction to deploy
- Recommends VPNs, password managers, and Totem Project for journalist digital safety training
- SLAPPs (Strategic Lawsuits Against Public Participation) used as harassment tool in democracies
- Online disinformation campaigns serve as precursors to real-world violence against journalists
- 90% global impunity rate for journalist murders according to UNESCO data
- Women journalists face disproportionate gender-based online harassment and physical threats

# Israel Blocks 37 Aid Groups Over Staff Data Requirements, Citing Security

**TL;DR:** Israeli authorities are barring 37 international NGOs from Gaza/West Bank operations after they refused new registration requirements demanding staff lists and biodata, citing violations of humanitarian neutrality principles.

## Key Points

- 37 international NGOs face operational ban starting March 1, 2026 for refusing new Israeli registration requirements
- Government Resolution No. 2542 mandates organizations provide complete staff lists and personal biodata to Ministry of Diaspora Affairs
- Aid groups including Oxfam, MSF, Save the Children refuse citing humanitarian neutrality principles and data protection obligations
- Organizations argue requirements create surveillance mechanism and compromise staff safety in conflict zone
- Ban affects groups running 60% of field hospitals and all child malnutrition centers in Gaza
- Law gives Israeli government broad power to deny registration for 'public safety or state security' risks
- Only 27 organizations have completed approved registration under new requirements
- UN estimates closure would immediately shut one-third of remaining health facilities in Gaza

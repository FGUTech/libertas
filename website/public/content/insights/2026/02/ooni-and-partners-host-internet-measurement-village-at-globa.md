# OONI and Partners Host Internet Measurement Village at Global Gathering 2025

**TL;DR:** Four major internet measurement projects are hosting an 'OMG Village' at Global Gathering 2025 in Portugal to advance censorship monitoring and shutdown detection capabilities.

## Key Points

- OONI hosting Open Measurement Gathering (OMG) Village at Global Gathering 2025 in Estoril, Portugal
- Four core partners: OONI, Measurement Lab (M-Lab), Internet Outage Detection & Analysis (IODA), and Censored Planet
- Additional participants include Netalitica and Project Ainita hosting booths
- Focus on internet shutdown investigation, censorship detection tools, and open datasets
- Community space for researchers, activists, and builders working on network interference
- Collaboration between established measurement platforms strengthens censorship detection ecosystem
- Event provides opportunity for knowledge transfer on technical measurement methodologies

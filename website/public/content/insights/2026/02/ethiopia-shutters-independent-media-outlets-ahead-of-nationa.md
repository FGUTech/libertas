# Ethiopia Shutters Independent Media Outlets Ahead of National Elections

**TL;DR:** Ethiopian authorities revoked the license of Addis Standard, one of the country's last independent news outlets, three months before national elections, escalating systematic media suppression.

## Key Points

- Ethiopia's Media Authority revoked operating license of Addis Standard on February 24, 2026
- Action taken 3 months before scheduled national elections
- Addis Standard was one of Ethiopia's last remaining independent media outlets since 2011
- Government cited unspecified violations of laws and media ethics, endangering national interests
- Editor-in-chief Yonas Kedir denied allegations, said no formal prior violations were communicated
- Part of escalating crackdown: Deutsche Welle journalists suspended, Wazema Radio license revoked, Reuters accreditation declined
- Pattern follows government raids, detentions, and harassment of independent journalists throughout 2025-2026
- Demonstrates urgent need for censorship-resistant communication infrastructure in authoritarian contexts

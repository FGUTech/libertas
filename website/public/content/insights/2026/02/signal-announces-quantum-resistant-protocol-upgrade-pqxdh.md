# Signal Announces Quantum-Resistant Protocol Upgrade (PQXDH)

**TL;DR:** Signal Protocol introduces PQXDH upgrade, adding quantum-resistant cryptography layer to protect against future quantum computers that could break current encryption.

## Key Points

- Signal Protocol announces first quantum resistance upgrade called PQXDH
- Upgrades existing X3DH key agreement protocol with quantum-resistant protections
- Signal Protocol used by billions globally across Signal and other messaging platforms
- Addresses threat of future quantum computers breaking current encryption standards
- Technical whitepaper and specifications published for developer implementation
- Represents proactive step toward post-quantum cryptography for secure communications
- Critical for long-term protection of activist and dissident communications

# Meta Considers Facial Recognition for Smart Glasses Despite $7B Privacy Settlement History

**TL;DR:** Meta is reportedly planning facial recognition for smart glasses, timing launch during political distractions. EFF warns of mass surveillance risks and billions in legal costs.

## Key Points

- Meta considering facial recognition for smart glasses according to internal documents [New York Times]
- Company plans launch "during dynamic political environment" to avoid civil society scrutiny
- Would require collecting faceprints from everyone in camera view without consent
- Meta previously paid nearly $7 billion in facial recognition privacy settlements
- Dozens of state laws require affirmative consent for biometric data collection
- EFF warns of mass surveillance, data breach, and discrimination risks
- Public backlash already seen with ICE's Mobile Fortify and Amazon Ring facial recognition

# Citizen Lab Submits UN Reports on Surveillance Tech Enabling Enforced Disappearances

**TL;DR:** Citizen Lab researchers submitted two reports to UN bodies detailing how targeted surveillance technology facilitates enforced disappearances and transnational repression of migrants.

## Key Points

- Citizen Lab co-authored two submissions to UN Committee on Enforced Disappearances
- First submission focuses on digital tools enabling state-sponsored disappearances
- Report calls on host states to protect against digital transnational repression
- Second submission examines how migration processes increase vulnerability to repression
- Authors highlight risks from inadequate privacy safeguards and data sharing agreements
- Both reports emphasize host state responsibility to protect vulnerable populations
- Submissions provide policy recommendations for addressing surveillance-enabled rights violations

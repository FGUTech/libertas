# CPJ Report: 58 of 59 Palestinian Journalists Report Torture in Israeli Detention

**TL;DR:** Committee to Protect Journalists releasing report Feb 19 documenting systematic torture claims from 58 of 59 Palestinian journalists detained by Israel since Oct 2023.

## Key Points

- CPJ conducted in-depth interviews with 59 Palestinian journalists released since October 7, 2023
- 58 of 59 journalists reported experiencing torture, abuse, or violence in Israeli custody
- Alleged abuses include beatings, starvation, medical neglect, sexual violence including rape
- Journalists describe stress positions, prolonged high-volume sound exposure, and threats
- CPJ reviewed supporting evidence including photographs, medical reports, and legal documents
- Systematic targeting of journalists represents critical threat to information freedom
- Report demonstrates how physical suppression of media workers enables information control
- Pattern suggests coordinated effort to silence Palestinian media coverage

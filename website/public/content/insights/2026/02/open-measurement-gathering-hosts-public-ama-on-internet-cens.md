# Open Measurement Gathering Hosts Public AMA on Internet Censorship Detection Tools

**TL;DR:** Key internet measurement organizations (OONI, Censored Planet, IODA, Measurement Lab) held public AMA sharing project updates and future plans for detecting censorship and network interference.

## Key Points

- Open Measurement Gathering (OMG) held public virtual event June 25, 2025
- Featured four major internet measurement organizations: OONI, Censored Planet, IODA, and Measurement Lab
- Each group presented 30-minute updates on platforms, tools, and datasets
- Event aimed to gather community feedback on censorship detection infrastructure
- Third OMG convening, following two previous private meetings with public reports
- Organizations provide critical data on internet shutdowns and censorship globally
- Community engagement suggests coordination among measurement infrastructure providers

# Discord Rolls Out Mandatory Age Verification Despite Data Breach History

**TL;DR:** Discord begins global mandatory age verification requiring government ID or face scans for many users, despite winning EFF's 2025 'Breachie Award' for exposing 70k users' identity documents in a hack.

## Key Points

- Discord launching mandatory age verification globally starting March 2026, not required by law in most jurisdictions
- Users estimated under 18 or with insufficient data face restrictions unless they upload government ID or face scan
- Third-party vendors k-ID (global) and Persona (UK) now handle verification after 2025 breach exposed 70,000 users' documents
- EFF warns this creates surveillance infrastructure and chills anonymous speech for vulnerable groups
- Users locked into 'teen experience' face content filters, DM restrictions, and blocked community features
- Age inference system uses account tenure, device data, and platform patterns to automatically sort most adults
- Facial age estimation technology is 'notoriously unreliable' especially for marginalized groups [EFF]
- Platform promises to delete IDs and keep face scans on-device, but users have no independent verification

# Iranian Prayer App Hacked to Send Surrender Messages During Israeli Strikes

**TL;DR:** Iranians received push notifications promising amnesty and saying 'help is on the way' through a compromised prayer app during Israeli airstrikes on Tehran.

## Key Points

- Iranian users received mysterious push notifications during Israeli airstrikes on Tehran
- Messages promised amnesty and stated 'help is on the way' if recipients surrendered
- Notifications delivered through a compromised prayer app
- Demonstrates weaponization of civilian digital infrastructure during conflict
- Highlights vulnerability of centralized apps to hostile takeover
- Shows how push notification systems can be used for psychological operations
- Exposes lack of user control over notifications on their own devices

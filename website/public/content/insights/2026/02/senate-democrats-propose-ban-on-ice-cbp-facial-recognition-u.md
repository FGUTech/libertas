# Senate Democrats Propose Ban on ICE/CBP Facial Recognition Use

**TL;DR:** New Senate bill would prohibit ICE and CBP from using facial recognition and other biometric surveillance, requiring deletion of existing data and allowing civil suits for violations.

## Key Points

- Senate Democrats introduced 'ICE Out of Our Faces Act' targeting federal immigration agencies
- Would ban ICE and CBP from acquiring, possessing, or using biometric surveillance systems
- Covers facial recognition, voice recognition, and other biometric technologies
- Requires deletion of all previously collected biometric surveillance data
- Prohibits use of biometric data in federal court cases or investigations
- Creates private right of action for individuals to sue for damages
- Allows state attorneys general to bring suits on behalf of residents
- Represents significant pushback against expanding federal surveillance infrastructure

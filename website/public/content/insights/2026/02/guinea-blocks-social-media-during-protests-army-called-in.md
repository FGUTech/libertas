# Guinea Blocks Social Media During Protests, Army Called In

**TL;DR:** Guinea restricted Facebook, WhatsApp, Instagram, TikTok and other platforms on May 17, 2023 as authorities deployed army to suppress planned protests.

## Key Points

- NetBlocks confirmed social media restrictions across Guinea on May 17, 2023
- Facebook, WhatsApp, Instagram, TikTok blocked on Orange and MTN networks
- Restrictions coincide with government calling in army to assist police against protests
- Measurements taken from 860 data points across 18 vantage points in Guinea
- NetBlocks notes VPN services can circumvent these government censorship measures
- Government cites maintaining public order as justification for restrictions
- Pattern follows previous internet shutdowns during Guinea's elections and political events
- Disruptions impact fundamental rights including freedom of expression and assembly

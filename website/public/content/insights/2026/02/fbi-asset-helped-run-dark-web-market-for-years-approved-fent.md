# FBI Asset Helped Run Dark Web Market for Years, Approved Fentanyl Sales

**TL;DR:** FBI secretly controlled a staffer at Incognito dark web market who approved sales of fentanyl-laced drugs, revealing extent of law enforcement infiltration of anonymous marketplaces.

## Key Points

- FBI asset was embedded as staffer within Incognito dark web marketplace operations
- Asset reportedly approved sales of fentanyl-tainted pills over extended period
- Sales included drugs from dealer linked to confirmed overdose death
- Reveals FBI's willingness to allow harmful drug sales to maintain operational cover
- Demonstrates sophisticated law enforcement infiltration of Tor-based markets
- Raises questions about liability for deaths occurring during FBI operations
- Shows dark web market operators face threats from both exit scams and law enforcement control

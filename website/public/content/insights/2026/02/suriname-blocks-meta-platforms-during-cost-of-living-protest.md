# Suriname Blocks Meta Platforms During Cost of Living Protests

**TL;DR:** Suriname restricted Facebook, WhatsApp, Instagram and Messenger on government network Telesur during February 2023 protests when demonstrators stormed parliament over economic crisis.

## Key Points

- NetBlocks confirmed restriction of Meta platforms (Facebook, WhatsApp, Instagram, Messenger) in Suriname February 17-18, 2023
- Blocks implemented on government-owned telecom network Telesur (AS27775) during cost of living protests
- Protesters stormed National Assembly in Paramaribo amid economic crisis and currency devaluation
- Social media restrictions lasted from Friday evening through Saturday morning
- NetBlocks verified disruption using real-time metrics from 100 measurement points across 10 vantage points
- VPN services confirmed as effective workaround for circumventing restrictions
- Exceptional measure for Suriname, which lacks significant internet censorship history
- Government condemned protests and established task force to manage situation

# Access Now Launches Spear Phishing Defense Webinar Series for Civil Society

**TL;DR:** Access Now's Digital Security Helpline is launching a webinar series on March 10, 2026, starting with spear phishing defense training for activists, journalists, and civil society organizations.

## Key Points

- Digital Security Helpline organizing 'Safe and Strong' webinar series starting March 10, 2026
- First session focuses on spear phishing attacks targeting high-profile activists and journalists
- Will cover practical examples from documented attacks in Eastern Europe and Middle East
- Expert speakers include Citizen Lab researcher Rebekah Brown and Lookout threat researcher Justin Albrecht
- Training addresses increasingly complex digital attacks against civil society organizations
- Series aims to build resilience through actionable threat prevention and response insights
- Registration available for 2 PM UTC session covering tailored social engineering techniques

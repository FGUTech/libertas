# Azerbaijani Journalist Under Armed Surveillance in Switzerland

**TL;DR:** Exiled Azerbaijani journalist Emin Huseynov reports being followed by armed men in Switzerland, highlighting transnational repression risks for freedom tech builders and activists.

## Key Points

- Emin Huseynov, co-founder of Institute for Reporters' Freedom and Safety, reports surveillance by armed men in Geneva
- Huseynov filmed one incident, posted footage to YouTube as evidence
- Swiss police investigating multiple surveillance episodes reported by Huseynov
- His brother Mehman Huseynov publishing critical videos of Azerbaijani president, living under police protection
- Pattern follows previous Azerbaijani state-linked assassination attempts against exiled journalist Mahammad Mirzali
- 2017 precedent: journalist Afgan Mukhtarli abducted from Georgia, linked to Azerbaijani authorities
- Demonstrates operational security failures and need for better protection protocols for exiled activists
- Azerbaijan ranks as world's 6th worst jailer of journalists with 24 currently imprisoned

# OONI Launches Community Survey to Improve Censorship Measurement Tools

**TL;DR:** OONI, the global internet censorship measurement network, is surveying its 50+ partner organizations and hundreds of thousands of users to improve their tools and outreach.

## Key Points

- OONI (Open Observatory of Network Interference) launched community survey
- Network includes over 50 partner organizations worldwide
- Hundreds of thousands of OONI Probe users contribute measurements globally
- Survey aims to improve outreach, tools, and documentation
- Community contributes through censorship measurements, localization, and research
- Feedback will inform development of critical internet freedom infrastructure
- OONI remains primary source for documenting global internet censorship

# Reports: Binance Investigators Found Crypto Flows to Iran, Company Denies Firings

**TL;DR:** Unverified reports claim Binance internal investigators discovered substantial crypto flows to Iran through user accounts, though Binance denies firing investigators over the findings.

## Key Points

- Unverified reports allege Binance investigators found significant crypto flows to Iran
- Flows reportedly occurred through regular Binance user accounts
- Binance has denied firing internal investigators related to these findings
- Iran faces extensive financial sanctions from US and other jurisdictions
- Crypto exchanges face regulatory pressure to enforce sanctions compliance
- Limited details available on scale, timeframe, or verification of alleged flows
- Story highlights ongoing tension between crypto accessibility and sanctions enforcement

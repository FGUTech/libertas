# Access Now Launches Digital Security Training Series for Civil Society

**TL;DR:** Access Now's Digital Security Helpline is hosting webinar series starting March 2026, focusing on spear-phishing defense for activists, journalists, and civil society facing targeted attacks.

## Key Points

- Access Now launching 'Safe and Strong' webinar series on digital security for civil society
- First session March 10, 2026 focuses on spear-phishing attacks targeting activists and journalists
- Training draws from documented attack cases in Eastern Europe and Middle East regions
- Expert speakers include Citizen Lab researcher, Lookout threat analyst, and regional security practitioners
- Series addresses increasing complexity of digital attacks against civil society organizations
- Content specifically tailored for high-risk users including human rights defenders
- Training includes practical examples and best practices for prevention and incident response

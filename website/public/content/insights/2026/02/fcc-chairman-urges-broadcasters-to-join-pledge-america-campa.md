# FCC Chairman Urges Broadcasters to Join 'Pledge America Campaign' for Pro-America Programming

**TL;DR:** FCC Chairman Brendan Carr launched a campaign asking broadcasters to voluntarily air patriotic programming including daily Pledge of Allegiance and civic education content for Trump's America 250th celebration.

## Key Points

- FCC Chairman Brendan Carr established 'Pledge America Campaign' tied to Trump's 'Salute to America 250' project
- Campaign asks broadcasters to voluntarily pledge to air 'patriotic, pro-America programming'
- Suggested content includes daily Pledge of Allegiance, Star Spangled Banner, civic education PSAs
- Examples include American history segments, patriotic music, and National Park Service site features
- Framed as voluntary and 'non-partisan' despite explicit political ties
- Represents potential soft editorial pressure from federal communications regulator
- Raises concerns about government influence over media content decisions

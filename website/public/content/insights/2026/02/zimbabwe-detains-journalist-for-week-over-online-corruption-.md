# Zimbabwe Detains Journalist for Week Over Online Corruption Report

**TL;DR:** Zimbabwean authorities detained Bulawayo24 journalist Gideon Madzikatidze for a week on cyberbullying and broadcasting charges after he published online corruption allegations against waste management company.

## Key Points

- Journalist Gideon Madzikatidze detained since February 18 on cyberbullying and broadcasting without license charges
- Bulawayo24 reporter published story alleging Geo Pomona Waste Management bribed journalists to avoid scrutiny
- Faces up to 10 years prison for cyberbullying under Criminal Law Act, 3 months under Broadcasting Services Act
- Authorities charging him under broadcast licensing law despite story only appearing on website
- Committee to Protect Journalists calls detention 'totally disproportionate response' to legitimate reporting
- Case demonstrates how cybercrime and licensing laws criminalize online journalism in Zimbabwe
- Pattern shows regulatory capture of internet publishing under traditional broadcast licensing framework

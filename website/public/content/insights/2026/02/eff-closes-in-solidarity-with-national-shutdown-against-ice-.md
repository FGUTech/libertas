# EFF Closes in Solidarity with National Shutdown Against ICE/CBP Actions

**TL;DR:** Electronic Frontier Foundation is closing Friday, Jan 30 to join national shutdown opposing ICE/CBP enforcement, while providing protest security resources.

## Key Points

- EFF announced office closure Friday, Jan 30 for national shutdown opposing ICE/CBP actions
- Organization expressing solidarity with Minneapolis communities and those impacted by federal enforcement
- EFF describes ongoing ICE/CBP activities as 'violence,' 'brutality and terror'
- Foundation providing practical security resources for protesters and activists
- Links provided to Surveillance Self-Defense guide for protest attendance
- Resources include guidance on legal right to record police/ICE activity
- Represents major digital rights organization taking direct activist stance

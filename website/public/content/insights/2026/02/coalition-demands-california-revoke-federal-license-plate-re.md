# Coalition Demands California Revoke Federal License Plate Reader Permits

**TL;DR:** EFF and 25+ organizations demand California revoke permits allowing CBP and DEA to deploy automated license plate readers disguised as traffic barrels along border highways.

## Key Points

- Coalition led by EFF and Imperial Valley Equity & Justice sent letter to Gov. Newsom and Caltrans Director
- Demands revocation of permits for federal ALPR surveillance on California highways
- CBP, DEA, and Border Patrol using ALPRs disguised as traffic barrels for mass surveillance
- Coalition cites risks of harassment, detention, and deportation for residents and migrants
- 25+ organizations signed letter including ACLU California Action, Fight for the Future, EPIC
- Timing linked to return of USBP Chief Greg Bovino to El Centro sector
- State-level pushback against federal surveillance infrastructure deployment

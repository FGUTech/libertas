# Access Now Launches Digital Security Webinar Series Targeting Civil Society Spear-Phishing Threats

**TL;DR:** Access Now's Digital Security Helpline launches webinar series starting March 2026, focusing on spear-phishing defense for activists and civil society with expert speakers from Citizen Lab and security industry.

## Key Points

- Access Now announces 'Safe and Strong' webinar series on digital security starting March 10, 2026
- First session focuses on spear-phishing attacks targeting activists, journalists, and civil society
- Expert speakers include Citizen Lab's Rebekah Brown and threat researchers from Lookout
- Training draws from documented attack cases in Eastern Europe and Middle East regions
- Series aims to share actionable threat prevention and response insights
- Initiative brings together civil society, private sector, and support organizations
- Content specifically addresses increasingly sophisticated social engineering techniques

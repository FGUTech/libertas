# ICE Detains Journalist Reporting on Immigration Enforcement

**TL;DR:** ICE detained Colombian journalist Estefany Rodríguez who covered immigration enforcement for Nashville Notícias, with legal team claiming retaliation for her reporting.

## Key Points

- ICE detained journalist Estefany Rodríguez on March 4 in Nashville without initial warrant
- Rodríguez covered ICE activities for Nashville Notícias, legal team claims detention is retaliation
- Journalist fled Colombia in 2021 after death threats, has pending asylum case and work authorization
- 40+ press freedom organizations demand her release from ICE custody
- Part of documented pattern of press suppression under second Trump administration
- Similar case: journalist Mario Guevara was deported to El Salvador despite legal status
- Habeas corpus hearing scheduled for March 17
- Demonstrates escalating government pressure on immigration reporting

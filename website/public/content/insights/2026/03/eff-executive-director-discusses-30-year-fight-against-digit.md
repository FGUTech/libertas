# EFF Executive Director Discusses 30-Year Fight Against Digital Surveillance in New Book

**TL;DR:** EFF's Cindy Cohn hosts livestream March 19th to discuss her new book 'Privacy's Defender' covering three decades of legal battles against NSA, FBI, and Justice Department surveillance.

## Key Points

- EFF Executive Director Cindy Cohn releasing book 'Privacy's Defender: My Thirty-Year Fight Against Digital Surveillance'
- Free livestream event March 19th, 11 AM-12 PM Pacific with 404 Media's Jason Koebler
- Book chronicles legal battles against Justice Department, NSA, and FBI over digital privacy rights
- Cohn led landmark Bernstein v. Dept. of Justice case challenging crypto export restrictions in 1993
- Central question addressed: Can private conversations exist when living online?
- Event includes live Q&A session and will be recorded for later viewing
- Part of EFF's 'EFFecting Change' livestream series marking organization's advocacy work

# Federal Agents Arrest Independent Photographer, Seize Equipment Over ICE Protest Coverage

**TL;DR:** DOJ charged independent photographer Shane Ryan Bollman with felony civil rights violations for covering an anti-ICE protest, with HSI agents seizing his devices without warrant.

## Key Points

- Federal agents arrested independent photographer Shane Ryan Bollman (Junn Bollmann) in Los Angeles on February 27
- Charged with felony 'conspiring to deprive others of their civil rights' (up to 10 years) plus FACE Act misdemeanor over January 18 anti-ICE protest coverage in Minnesota
- Homeland Security Investigations confiscated phone, camera, and laptop without providing search warrant
- Same charges filed against former CNN reporter Don Lemon and independent reporter Georgia Fort
- Committee to Protect Journalists calls pattern of using 'federal force to undermine journalists' First Amendment rights'
- Rare escalation: federal felony charges against journalists 'extremely rare' according to CPJ
- Part of broader crackdown with 30 total arrests connected to church demonstration during Sunday service
- Devices not returned upon release, creating operational security concerns for journalist sources

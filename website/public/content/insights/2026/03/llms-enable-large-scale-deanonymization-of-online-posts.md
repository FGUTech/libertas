# LLMs Enable Large-Scale Deanonymization of Online Posts

**TL;DR:** New research shows LLM agents can identify anonymous users across platforms like Reddit and Hacker News with high precision, scaling to tens of thousands of candidates by inferring location, occupation, and interests.

## Key Points

- LLM agents successfully deanonymize users from anonymous posts across multiple platforms
- Method works on Hacker News, Reddit, LinkedIn, and anonymized interview transcripts
- System scales to tens of thousands of potential candidate matches
- LLMs infer location, occupation, and interests from handful of comments
- Automated approach overcomes previous practical limitations of manual investigation
- Research demonstrates shift from theoretical to practical deanonymization threat
- Reported by Bruce Schneier with links to academic research paper

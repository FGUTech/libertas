# Access Now Maps 450 Companies Building EU's Migration Surveillance Infrastructure

**TL;DR:** Access Now investigation reveals 450+ private companies pitched 600+ surveillance technologies to EU migration agencies, creating mass surveillance system targeting migrants with limited oversight.

## Key Points

- Access Now mapped 450+ private companies pitching surveillance tech to EU migration agencies since 2017
- Three key agencies involved: eu-LISA (databases), Frontex (border control), Europol (law enforcement)
- Over 600 technological solutions pitched across biometrics, airborne surveillance, and AI/big data categories
- Private sector directly influences EU surveillance capabilities through closed-door pitch events
- Relationship between agencies and companies intentionally shielded from public scrutiny
- Affects millions of migrants through biometric collection and activity monitoring without consent
- Research highlights lack of transparency in procurement deals using public funds
- Demonstrates systematic privacy violations enabled by public-private surveillance partnerships

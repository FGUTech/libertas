# Panama Court Order Blocks Journalists from Reporting on Sanctioned Contractors

**TL;DR:** Panamanian prosecutor issued 4-month gag order against journalists investigating state contractors later sanctioned by US Treasury for supporting Maduro regime.

## Key Points

- Panamanian prosecutor issued 'protection order' blocking journalists Sabrina Bacal and Roberto Deniz from reporting on Carretero brothers
- Order stems from $1M defamation lawsuit filed by Roberto and Ramón Carretero Napolitano
- Journalists reported on brothers' business ties to multiple Panamanian administrations and Venezuela dealings
- US Treasury OFAC sanctioned both brothers in December 2025 for supporting Maduro regime
- Committee to Protect Journalists calls order 'flagrant violation of international freedom of expression standards'
- Control hearing scheduled to determine if order violates constitutional protections
- Case demonstrates how judicial systems can be weaponized to silence corruption reporting

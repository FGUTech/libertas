# Access Now Launches Digital Security Training Series for Civil Society

**TL;DR:** Access Now's Digital Security Helpline is launching a webinar series starting March 10, 2026, focusing on spear phishing defense for activists, journalists, and civil society organizations facing targeted attacks.

## Key Points

- Access Now announces 'Safe and Strong' webinar series on digital security for civil society
- First session March 10, 2026 focuses on spear phishing attacks targeting activists and journalists
- Training will use real-world examples from documented attacks in Eastern Europe and Middle East
- Expert speakers include researchers from Citizen Lab, Lookout, and Digital Security Lab Ukraine
- Aims to address increasingly complex digital attacks against civil society organizations
- Registration available through Access Now's Digital Security Helpline platform
- Part of broader initiative to increase digital resilience for at-risk communities

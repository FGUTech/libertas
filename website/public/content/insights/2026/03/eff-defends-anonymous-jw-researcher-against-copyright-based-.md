# EFF Defends Anonymous JW Researcher Against Copyright-Based Doxing Attempt

**TL;DR:** Watch Tower Bible and Tract Society is using DMCA subpoenas to unmask an anonymous Jehovah's Witness who created research tools exposing the organization's suppressed documents and failed prophecies.

## Key Points

- Watch Tower sent DMCA subpoenas to Google and Cloudflare seeking to identify anonymous researcher 'J. Doe'
- Doe created JWS Library website with research tools analyzing Jehovah's Witnesses historical documents
- Research revealed failed prophecies, erasure of leader disgrace, and suppressed church documents
- Watch Tower claims copyright infringement; EFF argues research constitutes clear fair use
- Doe remains anonymous to avoid 'disfellowshipping' retaliation that would sever family/social ties
- EFF filed motions to quash subpoenas in Southern District of New York
- Case demonstrates copyright law being weaponized to silence religious dissent and research
- First Amendment protects anonymous speech, especially when speakers face retribution

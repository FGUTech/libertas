# CPJ Documents Systematic ICE Targeting of Journalists During Second Trump Administration

**TL;DR:** Immigration authorities detained Tennessee journalist Estefany Rodríguez despite legal status, part of documented pattern targeting press covering ICE activities and immigration issues.

## Key Points

- ICE detained journalist Estefany Rodríguez in Nashville without warrant despite legal status
- Rodríguez reported on ICE activities for Nashville Notícias, sought asylum after Colombia death threats
- Part of systematic pattern: multiple journalists charged, deported, or denied entry since 2025
- Journalists covering anti-ICE protests specifically targeted by immigration enforcement
- Border officials conducting device searches and political interrogations of journalists
- CPJ issued first-ever US travel advisory for journalists due to electronic device searches
- Pattern includes deportation of legal residents and asylum seekers documenting government activities

# EFF's Cindy Cohn Releases Book on 30-Year Fight Against Digital Surveillance

**TL;DR:** EFF Executive Director Cindy Cohn published 'Privacy's Defender,' documenting three decades of legal battles against government surveillance including the Crypto Wars and NSA dragnet programs.

## Key Points

- Cindy Cohn published 'Privacy's Defender: My Thirty-Year Fight Against Digital Surveillance' through MIT Press
- Book covers foundational privacy battles including Crypto Wars, NSA surveillance, and FBI gag orders
- Describes legal fights that shaped internet freedom, free speech, and privacy rights
- Featured in conversation with Cory Doctorow at San Francisco's City Lights Bookstore
- National book tour planned with stops in major US cities
- EFF developing special podcast series featuring key players and moments from the book
- References landmark cases including Bernstein v. DOJ, Hepting v. AT&T, and Jewel v. NSA

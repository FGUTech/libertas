# Columbia University Failed to Protect Students from Warrantless ICE Raids Despite Clear Policies

**TL;DR:** ICE agents repeatedly entered Columbia University housing without warrants using deception, with school security failing to enforce policies requiring judicial warrants for non-public areas.

## Key Points

- ICE agents arrested neuroscience student Elmina Aghayeva from Columbia housing after deceiving security by claiming to search for missing child
- Columbia has explicit policy requiring judicial warrants for law enforcement entry to non-public school property since March 2025
- School security and staff repeatedly failed to enforce warrant requirements despite clear policies
- Palestinian student Mahmoud Khalil previously arrested by ICE from university housing without warrant after leading Gaza protests
- University showed differential response - quickly mobilized resources for Aghayeva but provided no support for Palestinian students
- Pattern shows multiple warrantless entries into university buildings over past year
- Columbia invested more in training security to suppress student protests than protect students from federal agents

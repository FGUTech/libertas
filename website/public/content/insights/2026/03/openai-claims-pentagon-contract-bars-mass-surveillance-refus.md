# OpenAI Claims Pentagon Contract Bars Mass Surveillance, Refuses to Release Proof

**TL;DR:** OpenAI secured a Pentagon AI contract after rival Anthropic was booted for refusing surveillance terms, but won't release contract details to verify claims about domestic spying prohibitions.

## Key Points

- OpenAI announced Pentagon contract after Anthropic talks collapsed over surveillance/weapons restrictions
- Anthropic was ordered to phase out government tools within 6 months for refusing mass surveillance terms
- OpenAI claims contract prohibits domestic mass surveillance and autonomous weapons use
- Company refuses to release actual contract language despite public scrutiny
- CEO Sam Altman says deal includes stricter protections and bars NSA collaboration
- Former Pentagon officials express skepticism about claimed restrictions
- OpenAI spokesperson made false claims about Pentagon's legal authority for data collection
- Contract language reportedly includes vague caveats around 'intentional' surveillance

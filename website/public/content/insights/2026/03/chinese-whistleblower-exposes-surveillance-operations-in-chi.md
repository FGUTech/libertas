# Chinese Whistleblower Exposes Surveillance Operations in China and US

**TL;DR:** A Chinese whistleblower has revealed details about China's surveillance operations targeting citizens domestically and in the United States, according to CNN reporting.

## Key Points

- Chinese whistleblower disclosed information about state surveillance operations [CNN via Freedom House]
- Surveillance targets include Chinese citizens both domestically and in the US
- Story involves China's United Front Work Department (UFWD) operations
- Revelations provide insight into Chinese intelligence gathering methods
- Exposes cross-border surveillance capabilities and reach

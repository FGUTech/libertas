# EFF Executive Director Releases Privacy Rights Memoir 'Privacy's Defender'

**TL;DR:** EFF's Cindy Cohn is launching a book chronicling her 30-year battle for digital privacy rights, covering crypto wars, surveillance state resistance, and EFF's evolution into a major digital rights organization.

## Key Points

- Cindy Cohn, EFF Executive Director, releasing 'Privacy's Defender' memoir in March 2026
- Book covers 30-year fight for digital privacy rights and chronicles crypto wars history
- Details EFF's growth from 'ragtag group of lawyers and hackers' to major digital rights org
- Cohn led successful First Amendment challenge to crypto export restrictions in Bernstein v. DOJ case
- Memoir addresses post-9/11 surveillance state and FBI gag orders
- Central question: 'Can we still have private conversations if we live our lives online?'
- Book launch event March 12, 2026 in Berkeley with conversation between Cohn and Annalee Newitz
- Proceeds benefit EFF's digital rights mission

# Lugano Expands Bitcoin Infrastructure with $6.3M Plan ₿ Phase II

**TL;DR:** Tether and Lugano launch Phase II expansion targeting digital sovereignty through decentralized infrastructure, privacy-preserving identity, and AI agents by 2030.

## Key Points

- Tether commits CHF 5M ($6.3M) for Lugano's Plan ₿ Phase II (2026-2030) digital infrastructure expansion
- Over 400 Lugano merchants now accept Bitcoin, USDT, and LVGA token after 4-year pilot program
- Five strategic pillars: institutional blockchain (SwissLedger), digital trade, privacy-preserving identity, decentralized AI, resilient urban infrastructure
- Privacy pillar uses zero-knowledge technologies for voluntary citizen/business verification
- PoW.space hub has attracted 100+ blockchain companies to the city
- Mayor explicitly frames initiative as preserving 'freedom' through data sovereignty and service autonomy
- Demonstrates municipal-scale adoption of freedom tech infrastructure in European context

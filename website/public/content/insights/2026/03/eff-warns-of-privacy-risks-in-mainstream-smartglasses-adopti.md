# EFF Warns of Privacy Risks in Mainstream Smartglasses Adoption

**TL;DR:** EFF highlights privacy concerns with Meta Ray-Bans and other mainstream smartglasses, warning that third parties may access recorded content beyond just device owners.

## Key Points

- Smartglasses with embedded cameras and microphones have reached mainstream adoption
- EFF warns that Meta Ray-Bans raise significant privacy concerns for users and bystanders
- Third parties can potentially access recorded content, not just device owners
- EFF recommends users 'think twice' before purchasing or using Meta's Ray-Bans
- Privacy implications extend beyond individual users to broader surveillance concerns
- EFF covering this in newsletter alongside Internet Archive blocking and new leadership
- Represents shift toward ubiquitous recording devices in public spaces

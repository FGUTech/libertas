# DRC Journalists Detained, Broadcasters Occupied as Warring Parties Control Information Flow

**TL;DR:** Government forces and M23 rebels in eastern DRC systematically detain journalists and occupy broadcast stations to control war coverage, creating information blackout zones.

## Key Points

- Multiple journalists detained by both DRC government forces and M23 rebels over war coverage in January-March 2026
- Serge Sindani (Kis24.info) arrested by intelligence agents, accused of being M23 spy for publishing rebel statements
- Patient Chimusa detained by military for interviewing rebel spokesperson, released on $500 bail
- M23 rebels physically occupied Walungu Community Radio, looting transmission equipment and establishing military positions
- Rebels forced at least three state broadcasters (RTNC) to only air pro-M23 content
- Pattern shows both sides systematically targeting media infrastructure to control information flow
- Creates information vacuum in conflict zones where independent reporting is criminalized by all parties

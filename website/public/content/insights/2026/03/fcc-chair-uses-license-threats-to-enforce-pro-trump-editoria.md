# FCC Chair Uses License Threats to Enforce Pro-Trump Editorial Control Over Broadcasters

**TL;DR:** FCC Chair Brendan Carr systematically threatens broadcast licenses to suppress coverage critical of Trump, targeting national stories while claiming to protect local interests.

## Key Points

- FCC Chair Brendan Carr threatens broadcast license revocations over coverage critical of Trump administration
- Targeted stations over Iranian missile strike coverage, immigration enforcement reporting, and Harris interview
- Used 'localism' rhetoric while actually policing national/international news content
- Delayed Paramount-Skydance merger approval until Trump received settlement payment from lawsuit
- Demanded transcripts from PBS/NPR over BBC documentary about January 6
- Threatened ABC over Jimmy Kimmel's commentary, using mafia-style language
- Launched 'Pledge America Campaign' demanding 'patriotic, pro-America content'
- Approved media consolidation deals with Trump-aligned conglomerates Nexstar and Sinclair
- Created chilling effect leading to 'rampant self-censorship' at targeted stations

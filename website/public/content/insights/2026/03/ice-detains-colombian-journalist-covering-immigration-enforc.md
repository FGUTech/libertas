# ICE Detains Colombian Journalist Covering Immigration Enforcement in Nashville

**TL;DR:** ICE detained journalist Estefany Rodríguez, who covers immigration enforcement for Nashville Notícias, despite being in the US legally with pending asylum and green card applications.

## Key Points

- Estefany Rodríguez detained by ICE on March 4, 2026 without warrant while in her branded Nashville Notícias vehicle
- Rodríguez was in the US legally with pending asylum case and green card application through marriage
- Journalist fled Colombia in 2021 after receiving death threats for her reporting
- She covers Immigration and Customs Enforcement activity for Spanish-language outlet Nashville Notícias
- Committee to Protect Journalists calls this part of 'alarming pattern' under Trump administration
- Rodríguez being transferred to ICE processing center in Louisiana from Tennessee detention facility
- Case follows similar detention of Atlanta journalist Mario Guevara, who was deported despite legal status
- Demonstrates targeting of journalists covering sensitive government operations

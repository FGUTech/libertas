# Judge Blocks DOD Blacklisting of Anthropic as First Amendment Retaliation

**TL;DR:** Federal judge granted preliminary injunction blocking Department of War's blacklisting of Anthropic, ruling it was unconstitutional retaliation for the company's critical press statements.

## Key Points

- US District Judge Rita Lin blocked Department of War's blacklisting of Anthropic AI company
- Judge ruled the action constituted 'classic First Amendment retaliation'
- DOD designated Anthropic as supply-chain risk due to 'hostile manner through the press'
- Court found no evidence Anthropic posed urgent national security threat
- Officials failed to consider less restrictive alternatives before extreme measures
- Preliminary injunction granted, suggesting Anthropic likely to prevail on merits
- Sets precedent against government retaliation targeting AI companies for speech

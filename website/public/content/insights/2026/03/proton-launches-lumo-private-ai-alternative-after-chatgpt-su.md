# Proton Launches Lumo: Private AI Alternative After ChatGPT Surveillance Concerns

**TL;DR:** Proton released Lumo, a privacy-focused AI assistant with end-to-end encryption and no data logging, following reports that 4M users pledged to leave ChatGPT over government surveillance partnerships.

## Key Points

- Over 4 million users reportedly pledged to leave ChatGPT after controversial US government partnership raised surveillance fears
- Proton launched Lumo as private alternative with bidirectional asymmetric encryption for all conversations
- Lumo uses zero-access encrypted chat history - only user can access their data
- User conversations never used for AI training, unlike mainstream AI tools
- Hosted on European servers under EU jurisdiction, protecting from US warrantless surveillance
- Built on open-source models with transparent, auditable code
- No third-party data sharing, ads, or behavioral profiling - subscription-funded model

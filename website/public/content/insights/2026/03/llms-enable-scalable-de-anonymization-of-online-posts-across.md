# LLMs Enable Scalable De-anonymization of Online Posts Across Platforms

**TL;DR:** Research shows LLMs can identify anonymous users from their online posts with high precision across platforms like Hacker News and Reddit, scaling to tens of thousands of candidates.

## Key Points

- LLM agents successfully de-anonymize users from anonymous posts on Hacker News, Reddit, LinkedIn, and interview transcripts
- Method works at scale, testing against tens of thousands of potential candidates
- LLMs infer location, occupation, and interests from handful of comments, then search web for matches
- Breakthrough makes previously human-intensive de-anonymization practical and automated
- Reported by Bruce Schneier, indicating credible security research [Source]
- Threatens pseudonymous communication relied upon by activists and dissidents
- Represents major shift from theoretical to practical de-anonymization capabilities

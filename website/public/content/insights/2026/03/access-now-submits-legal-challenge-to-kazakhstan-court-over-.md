# Access Now Submits Legal Challenge to Kazakhstan Court Over 2022 Internet Shutdowns

**TL;DR:** Digital rights organization Access Now has filed a submission to Kazakhstan's Constitutional Court challenging the legality of internet shutdowns that occurred in 2022.

## Key Points

- Access Now filed legal submission to Constitutional Court of Kazakhstan
- Challenge focuses on internet shutdowns that occurred during 2022
- Kazakhstan experienced significant internet disruptions during January 2022 protests
- Legal challenge could set precedent for shutdown accountability in Central Asia
- Access Now specializes in documenting and legally challenging internet restrictions
- Constitutional court involvement suggests fundamental rights arguments being made
- Outcome could influence future government response to digital rights violations

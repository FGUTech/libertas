# EFF's Cindy Cohn on Daily Show to Discuss 30 Years of Privacy Advocacy

**TL;DR:** EFF Executive Director Cindy Cohn appears on The Daily Show to discuss her new book covering three decades fighting surveillance, from the Crypto Wars to NSA dragnet spying.

## Key Points

- Cindy Cohn, EFF Executive Director, featured on The Daily Show March 30
- Promoting new book 'Privacy's Defender: My Thirty-Year Fight Against Digital Surveillance' (MIT Press)
- Book covers her role in major digital rights battles including 1990s Crypto Wars
- Details resistance to NSA's dragnet internet surveillance programs revealed in 2000s
- Describes fights against FBI gag orders and support for whistleblowers
- Represents broader EFF work defending technologists, innovators, and researchers
- Mainstream media platform amplifies decades of freedom tech advocacy work

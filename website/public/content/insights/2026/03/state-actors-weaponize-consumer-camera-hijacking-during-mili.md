# State Actors Weaponize Consumer Camera Hijacking During Military Operations

**TL;DR:** New research reveals Iranian state hackers hijacked hundreds of consumer cameras coordinated with missile strikes, with Israel, Russia, and Ukraine adopting similar surveillance warfare tactics.

## Key Points

- Iranian state hackers conducted hundreds of consumer camera hijacking attempts
- Attacks timed to coincide with missile and drone strikes for intelligence gathering
- Israel, Russia, and Ukraine have also adopted camera hijacking as warfare tactic
- Consumer-grade security cameras being systematically compromised by state actors
- Represents evolution of surveillance warfare targeting civilian infrastructure
- Demonstrates vulnerability of IoT devices in conflict zones
- Shows state-level resources being used to exploit weak consumer security

# Taliban Shuts Down and Seizes Private TV Station in Afghanistan

**TL;DR:** Taliban intelligence raided and forcibly closed Rah-e-Farda TV in Kabul, seizing assets and expelling staff after owner criticized Taliban-Pakistan conflict.

## Key Points

- Taliban intelligence officers raided Rah-e-Farda TV's Kabul office on February 28, 2026
- Station forced off air, staff verbally abused and expelled from premises
- Assets confiscated by order of Taliban leader Hibatullah Akhundzada
- Closure triggered by owner Mohammad Mohaqiq's comments on Taliban-Pakistan conflict
- Fighting escalated after Pakistan conducted airstrikes in Afghanistan over militant allegations
- Pattern continues: Taliban previously seized Kawoon Ghag radio station in 2023
- Demonstrates systematic repression of independent media under Taliban rule

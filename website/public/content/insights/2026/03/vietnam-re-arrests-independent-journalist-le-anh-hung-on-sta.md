# Vietnam Re-Arrests Independent Journalist Le Anh Hung on State Propaganda Charges

**TL;DR:** Vietnamese authorities arrested independent journalist Le Anh Hung on March 9, charging him under Article 117 for 'propagandizing against the state' - his second arrest for online commentary critical of the government.

## Key Points

- Le Anh Hung arrested March 9 in Dak Lak province, charged under Article 117 carrying up to 20 years prison
- Hung contributes to independent outlets including Thien Dan and maintains personal blog
- Previously served 5-year sentence (2018-2023) under Article 331 for 'abuse of democratic freedoms'
- Endured torture including beatings and forced drug injections in psychiatric hospital [Radio Free Asia]
- Member of Independent Journalists Association of Vietnam, several members imprisoned
- Vietnam holds 17 journalists behind bars, among world's worst for press freedom [CPJ data]
- Case demonstrates state crackdown on independent online commentary and digital expression

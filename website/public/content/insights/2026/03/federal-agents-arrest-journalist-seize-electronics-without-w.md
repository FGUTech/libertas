# Federal Agents Arrest Journalist, Seize Electronics Without Warrant in ICE Protest Coverage

**TL;DR:** DOJ charged independent photographer Junn Bollmann with felony civil rights violations for covering anti-ICE protest, confiscating his devices without warrant—part of broader crackdown on press freedom.

## Key Points

- Independent photographer Shane Ryan Bollman (Junn Bollmann) arrested by HSI agents in Los Angeles February 27
- Charged with felony 'conspiring to deprive others of civil rights' for covering January 18 anti-ICE protest in St. Paul
- Federal agents confiscated phone, camera, laptop without providing search warrant
- Faces up to 10 years prison plus potential $10,000 fine under FACE Act misdemeanor
- Same charges filed against former CNN reporter Don Lemon and independent reporter Georgia Fort
- Part of 30-person federal indictment related to January church demonstration coverage
- CPJ calls pattern of using federal force to undermine First Amendment rights
- Devices not returned upon release, creating operational security concerns for journalist

# Tibet: 45 Detained for Digital Communications as China Intensifies Phone Surveillance

**TL;DR:** Chinese authorities detained 45 Tibetans in 2024-25, primarily for phone content including sharing information abroad or having Dalai Lama materials, with random phone searches as key surveillance tactic.

## Key Points

- 45 politically motivated detentions in Tibet during 2024-25, mostly for digital communications [HRW]
- 19 Tibetans punished for sending information abroad, sharing 'illegal' Dalai Lama content, or storing such material
- Random phone searches used as surveillance tactic - waitress detained after search, held year without charge
- 15 cases constitute enforced disappearances with unknown whereabouts, including three popular singers
- Two detainees died in custody, two died shortly after release
- Four senior religious figures detained, including operators of Tibetan language schools
- Mass arrests followed protests against dam construction and gold mining operations
- Almost no independent news escapes Tibet due to information controls

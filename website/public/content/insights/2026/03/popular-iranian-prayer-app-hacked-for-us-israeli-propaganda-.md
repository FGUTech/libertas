# Popular Iranian Prayer App Hacked for US-Israeli Propaganda Messages

**TL;DR:** A prayer app with 5M+ downloads was reportedly compromised by US/Israeli forces to send propaganda messages to Iranians during recent explosions, demonstrating state-level mobile app infiltration capabilities.

## Key Points

- BadeSaba Calendar prayer app with 5+ million downloads from Google Play was reportedly hacked
- App sent burst of messages to Iranian users during explosions, starting with 'Help has arrived'
- Messages delivered over 30-minute period beginning at 9:52 AM Tehran time
- No party officially claimed responsibility for the app compromise
- Schneier assesses this as likely US/Israeli government operation due to speed and coordination
- Demonstrates pre-positioning of access in popular mobile applications
- Shows how legitimate apps can become vectors for state propaganda campaigns
- Affects millions of users who trusted app for religious purposes

# Turkey Systematically Targets Opposition Leader İmamoğlu in Mass Trial

**TL;DR:** Istanbul mayor and presidential candidate Ekrem İmamoğlu faces politically motivated mass trial with 407 defendants, part of 17-month campaign to remove opposition leadership through weaponized justice system.

## Key Points

- Ekrem İmamoğlu, Istanbul mayor and CHP presidential candidate, stands trial March 9, 2026 on corruption charges
- Mass prosecution includes 407 defendants, mostly Istanbul Metropolitan Municipality employees
- İmamoğlu detained for one year, faces up to 1929 years in prison if convicted on all counts
- Evidence relies heavily on 15 'secret witnesses' and testimony from co-defendants seeking reduced sentences
- Timeline shows coordinated campaign: investigations opened day after presidential candidacy announcement
- CHP won 37.8% in 2024 local elections, surpassing Erdoğan's AKP (35.5%) for first time in 22 years
- Similar prosecutions target CHP mayors across multiple cities including Adana and Antalya
- Independent media covering İmamoğlu's arrest face fines, broadcast bans, and journalist detentions

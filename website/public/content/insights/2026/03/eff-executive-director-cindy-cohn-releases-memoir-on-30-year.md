# EFF Executive Director Cindy Cohn Releases Memoir on 30-Year Digital Privacy Fight

**TL;DR:** EFF Executive Director Cindy Cohn publishes memoir documenting three decades of digital privacy battles including Crypto Wars, NSA surveillance, and FBI gag orders as she prepares to step down.

## Key Points

- Cindy Cohn releasing memoir 'Privacy's Defender: My Thirty-Year Fight Against Digital Surveillance' via MIT Press
- Book covers Crypto Wars of 1990s, NSA dragnet surveillance battles, and fights against FBI gag orders
- Cohn stepping down as EFF Executive Director after 25 years with the organization
- National book tour launching with San Francisco and Berkeley events in March
- Book features endorsements from Edward Snowden, Lawrence Lessig, and Senator Ron Wyden
- Proceeds from book sales benefit EFF
- Documents legal battles representing technology users, innovators, whistleblowers, and researchers

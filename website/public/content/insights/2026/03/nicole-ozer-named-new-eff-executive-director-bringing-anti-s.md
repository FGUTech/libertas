# Nicole Ozer Named New EFF Executive Director, Bringing Anti-Surveillance Expertise

**TL;DR:** Nicole Ozer, architect of California's strongest electronic surveillance laws, will lead EFF starting June 1, succeeding Cindy Cohn after 25+ years with the organization.

## Key Points

- Nicole Ozer appointed as EFF's new executive director effective June 1, 2025
- Succeeds Cindy Cohn, who led EFF for nearly a decade and worked there 25+ years
- Ozer previously founding director of ACLU Northern California's Technology and Civil Liberties Program (2004-2025)
- Led passage of California Electronic Communications Privacy Act - nation's strongest warrant requirement for electronic data
- Created model surveillance oversight law adopted in 25+ jurisdictions protecting 17+ million people
- Developed campaigns against social media surveillance and facial recognition at major tech companies
- Brings two decades of litigation experience including Supreme Court cases
- Transition comes as EFF celebrates 35th anniversary and faces 'critical times' for digital rights

# EFF Director Cindy Cohn Discusses Privacy Book on The Daily Show

**TL;DR:** EFF Executive Director Cindy Cohn appeared on The Daily Show to discuss her new book covering 30 years of digital privacy battles including the Crypto Wars and NSA surveillance.

## Key Points

- Cindy Cohn appeared on The Daily Show with Jon Stewart on March 30
- Promoted her book 'Privacy's Defender: My Thirty-Year Fight Against Digital Surveillance'
- Book covers her role in the Crypto Wars of the 1990s
- Details battles against NSA's dragnet internet spying programs revealed in 2000s
- Discusses fights against FBI gag orders and digital surveillance overreach
- Represents EFF's advocacy for technology users, innovators, and whistleblowers
- Major media platform exposure for digital rights and privacy issues

# Netherlands Considers Jailbreaking F-35 Jets to Break US Software Dependency

**TL;DR:** Dutch Defense Secretary said he could jailbreak F-35 fighter jets to accept third-party software, highlighting growing concerns about US technological dependencies.

## Key Points

- Dutch Defense Secretary suggested jailbreaking F-35 fighter jets to run third-party software
- Countries worldwide expressing concern about dependencies on US technology
- F-35 purchasers currently dependent on US for all software maintenance
- Represents state-level application of censorship resistance principles
- Demonstrates how technological sovereignty affects even military hardware
- Pattern of nations seeking to break free from US platform control
- Shows jailbreaking concepts scaling beyond consumer devices to critical infrastructure

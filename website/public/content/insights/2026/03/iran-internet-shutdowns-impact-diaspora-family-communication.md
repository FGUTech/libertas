# Iran Internet Shutdowns Impact Diaspora Family Communications

**TL;DR:** Iranian internet restrictions are forcing diaspora families to rely on messaging app read receipts as vital signs of life for relatives inside the country.

## Key Points

- Iranian diaspora monitoring WhatsApp read receipts to confirm family safety
- Internet shutdowns in Iran disrupting routine family communications
- Read receipts serving as basic proof-of-life indicators during blackouts
- Communications restrictions creating anxiety for families abroad
- Pattern highlights human cost of internet censorship policies
- Demonstrates need for more resilient communication channels

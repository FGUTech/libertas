# Nawat Indigenous Language Gets Official Wikipedia After 16-Year Incubation

**TL;DR:** Wikipedia in Nawat, a critically endangered indigenous language of El Salvador, achieved official status in January 2026 after community mobilization focused on digital sovereignty and knowledge preservation.

## Key Points

- Nawat Wikipedia ('Wikiamachti') officially launched January 2026 after 16 years in Wikimedia incubator
- Nawat is classified as critically endangered by UNESCO with only 1,135 native speakers (2024 census)
- Language suppression intensified after 1932 La Matanza state repression targeting Indigenous speakers
- Revival led by 'Timumachtikan Nawat' collective providing technical training and community workshops
- Project now includes 1,000+ articles, joining other Latin American Indigenous Wikipedias
- Community views this as digital sovereignty - autonomous control over documenting local knowledge
- Addresses challenge of limited written sources by legitimizing oral tradition knowledge
- Global participation includes learners from Morocco taking virtual Nawat classes

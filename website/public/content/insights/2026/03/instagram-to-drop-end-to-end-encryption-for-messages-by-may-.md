# Instagram to Drop End-to-End Encryption for Messages by May 2026

**TL;DR:** Instagram will discontinue end-to-end encrypted messaging by May 8, 2026, marking a significant reversal of Meta's previous commitment to private communications.

## Key Points

- Instagram help pages confirm E2EE messaging support ends May 8, 2026
- Users must download encrypted chats and media before deadline or lose access
- Reversal contradicts Meta's 2019 strategy to expand encryption across all platforms
- WhatsApp and Messenger E2EE status remains unclear from this announcement
- No public explanation provided for why E2EE is being discontinued
- Change occurs amid renewed policy debates over encryption vs. content moderation
- TikTok also recently declined to implement E2EE citing safety concerns
- Raises questions about future Instagram chat privacy and Meta's data access

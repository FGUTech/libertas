# ICE Agents Now Checking IDs at TSA Checkpoints, Targeting Latinos

**TL;DR:** Trump deployed ICE agents to TSA checkpoints at US airports, where they're requesting additional ID from Latino passengers, potentially as a 'test run' for expanded surveillance operations.

## Key Points

- ICE agents have replaced TSA staff at JFK Airport security checkpoints for ID verification
- Agents selectively requesting second form of ID from Latino passengers while letting white passengers pass normally
- Steve Bannon suggested on his podcast this may be a 'test run' ahead of November midterms [The Intercept]
- TSA agents training ICE personnel on checkpoint procedures while working unpaid [The Intercept]
- First-hand account documents differential treatment based on apparent ethnicity/surname
- Pattern suggests normalization strategy for expanded federal agent presence in daily life
- Deployment represents escalation from immigration enforcement to broader population control

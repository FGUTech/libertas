# Taliban Seizes Private TV Station, Continues Media Crackdown in Afghanistan

**TL;DR:** Taliban intelligence raided and shut down Rah-e-Farda TV in Kabul, seizing assets and expelling staff, continuing systematic suppression of independent media in Afghanistan.

## Key Points

- Taliban intelligence officers raided Rah-e-Farda TV's Kabul office on February 28, 2026
- Staff were verbally abused and expelled, station forced off air by order of Taliban leader Hibatullah Akhundzada
- Station assets were confiscated and building seized by authorities
- Taliban claimed shutdown was due to owner Mohammad Mohaqiq's comments about Taliban-Pakistan conflict
- Follows 2023 pattern when Taliban seized Kawoon Ghag radio station in Laghman province
- Committee to Protect Journalists reports this as part of systematic media repression campaign
- Demonstrates escalating control over information infrastructure and independent reporting
- Creates environment where censorship-resistant communication tools become essential for journalists

# Bitcoin and Freedom Tech Hub Launches in Nairobi with Infrastructure Focus

**TL;DR:** Node NBO opens in Nairobi as Africa's first dedicated Bitcoin/freedom tech co-working space, featuring solar power, satellite internet paid in Bitcoin, and partnerships with HRF and key African Bitcoin companies.

## Key Points

- Node NBO launched May 16, 2026 in Nairobi's diplomatic district near UN headquarters
- Houses Fedi, Gridless, BTrust, Human Rights Foundation and other freedom tech organizations
- Features three labs: open-source energy, Bitcoin mining, and AI compute
- Facility powered entirely by solar panels installed by Gridless
- SateNet provides community-owned satellite internet paid with Bitcoin via Fedi app
- Serves underserved communities including Kenya's Kibera and South Africa's Mossel Bay
- Located near UN headquarters to showcase African Bitcoin projects to international visitors
- Follows Kenyan communal decision-making culture for Bitcoin community coordination

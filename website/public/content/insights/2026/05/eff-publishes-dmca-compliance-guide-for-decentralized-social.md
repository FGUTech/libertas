# EFF Publishes DMCA Compliance Guide for Decentralized Social Platform Operators

**TL;DR:** EFF released comprehensive guidance on copyright law compliance for Mastodon, Bluesky, and other decentralized social platform operators to protect against legal liability while maintaining user protections.

## Key Points

- EFF published DMCA best practices guide targeting decentralized social platform operators
- Covers Mastodon, ActivityPub servers, Bluesky hosts, RSS mirrors, and similar protocols
- Five key requirements: designate DMCA agent, respond to notices, have repeat infringer policy, address known infringement, avoid encouraging infringement
- Must register designated agent with US Copyright Office directory and renew every 3 years
- Operators can challenge invalid DMCA notices without losing safe harbor protections entirely
- Counter-notification process allows users to restore wrongfully removed content
- Emphasizes that hosts don't need to actively monitor for infringement, only respond when notified
- Legal infrastructure guidance critical for sustainability of decentralized platforms

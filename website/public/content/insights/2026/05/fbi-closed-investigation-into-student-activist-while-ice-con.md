# FBI Closed Investigation Into Student Activist While ICE Continued Detention

**TL;DR:** FBI documents reveal the agency closed investigation into Palestinian activist Mahmoud Khalil after determining he posed no threat, yet ICE continued detention based on anonymous tip claiming Hamas support.

## Key Points

- FBI received anonymous tip March 6, 2025 claiming Columbia student Mahmoud Khalil called for 'violence on behalf of Hamas'
- FBI closed investigation March 19, 2025 determining Khalil 'does not warrant further FBI investigation'
- ICE arrested Khalil March 8, 2025 and detained him in Louisiana despite FBI findings
- Trump administration continued labeling Khalil as 'Hamas supporter' after FBI cleared him
- Documents obtained through FOIA lawsuit by Center for Constitutional Rights
- Case demonstrates how anonymous tips can trigger multi-agency targeting of activists
- Part of broader Trump administration crackdown on pro-Palestine student speech
- Shows disconnect between surveillance findings and immigration enforcement actions

# Burkina Faso Junta Bans TV5Monde, Escalates Information Control Campaign

**TL;DR:** Burkina Faso's military government suspended French broadcaster TV5Monde for 'disinformation,' continuing systematic media crackdown since 2022 coup including journalist disappearances and coordinated online propaganda.

## Key Points

- Burkina Faso media regulator suspended TV5Monde on May 5, 2026 for alleged 'disinformation' and 'apology of terrorism'
- TV5Monde reaches millions across 200+ countries, previously suspended twice in 2024, also banned in Mali
- Part of broader crackdown since 2022 coup: independent media suspended, civil society dismantled, journalists detained
- Investigative journalist Atiana Serge Oulon forcibly disappeared June 2024, allegedly tortured, whereabouts unknown
- Government fills media vacuum with state-sponsored misinformation and propaganda machine
- Pro-junta 'Rapid Communication Intervention Battalions' coordinate social media messaging campaigns
- Digital groups promote President Ibrahim Traoré cult of personality while spreading anti-Fulani hate speech
- Systematic suppression creates environment where censorship-resistant tools become critical for information access

# OONI Report: Internet Censorship Becoming More Sophisticated and Targeted

**TL;DR:** OONI's network measurement data reveals internet censorship is evolving toward more sophisticated, targeted, and intermittent blocks that are harder to detect globally.

## Key Points

- Internet censorship becoming more sophisticated, targeted, and harder to detect [OONI]
- OONI's crowdsourced network measurement data supports censorship research and advocacy
- Longitudinal data reveals trend toward targeted, intermittent blocks for short-term control
- Long-term systematic blocks used for sustained suppression of information
- Evolution makes transparency and measurement infrastructure more critical
- Data enables rapid response to censorship events globally
- Shift from broad censorship to precision targeting complicates detection efforts

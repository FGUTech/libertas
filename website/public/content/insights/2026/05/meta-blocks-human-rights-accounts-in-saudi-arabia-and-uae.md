# Meta Blocks Human Rights Accounts in Saudi Arabia and UAE

**TL;DR:** Meta has reportedly blocked human rights organizations' accounts from reaching audiences in Saudi Arabia and UAE, demonstrating platform-level censorship in authoritarian regions.

## Key Points

- Meta blocked human rights accounts from reaching audiences in Saudi Arabia and UAE [Source]
- Access Now published press release documenting the censorship [Source]
- Demonstrates platform compliance with authoritarian government pressure
- Highlights vulnerability of centralized social media for human rights advocacy
- Pattern consistent with Meta's previous content restrictions in authoritarian regions
- Underscores need for decentralized communication platforms for activists

# Access Now Hosts Digital Security Webinar Series for Civil Society in Conflict Zones

**TL;DR:** Access Now's Digital Security Helpline is running a webinar series focusing on protecting civil society and human rights defenders from digital threats during armed conflicts and humanitarian crises.

## Key Points

- Access Now hosting 'Safe and Strong' webinar series on digital security for civil society
- May 19, 2026 session focuses on digital threats during armed conflict and humanitarian crises
- Speakers include experts from WITNESS, Cloudflare, Oxfam, and Access Now's Digital Security Helpline
- Series addresses spear-phishing attacks and advanced persistent threats against activists
- Previous session covered spear-phishing prevention with Citizen Lab and Lookout researchers
- Targets humanitarian organizations, human rights defenders, and support organizations
- Recognizes digital harms as component of modern warfare and conflict

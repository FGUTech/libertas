# Rights Groups Demand Microsoft Disclose Review of Israeli Military AI/Cloud Services

**TL;DR:** Five major rights organizations are pressuring Microsoft to publish findings of its review into Israeli military use of Azure and AI services for surveillance and targeting of Palestinians.

## Key Points

- Access Now, Amnesty, EFF, and others issued joint letter demanding Microsoft transparency
- Microsoft reportedly completed legal review of Israeli military's use of its technologies but hasn't released findings
- Media investigations revealed Azure/AI services used by Israeli intelligence Unit 8200 for mass surveillance
- Services allegedly processed intercepted Palestinian communications for lethal military operations in Gaza
- Microsoft announced restrictions on Unit 8200 but didn't specify which services were suspended
- Organizations warn of potential civil/criminal liability for complicity in international law violations
- Microsoft transferred Israeli branch management to France amid scrutiny over defense dealings
- Groups demand suspension of all contracts contributing to human rights abuses

# Senate Confirms Bitcoin-Friendly Kevin Warsh to Fed Board

**TL;DR:** Senate confirmed Kevin Warsh to Fed Board 51-45, clearing path to replace Jerome Powell as Fed Chair. Warsh calls bitcoin 'important asset' and 'good policeman for policy.'

## Key Points

- Senate confirmed Kevin Warsh to Federal Reserve Board of Governors in 51-45 party-line vote
- Confirmation clears path for final vote on Fed chairmanship to replace Jerome Powell
- Warsh publicly supports bitcoin, calling it 'an important asset' and 'very good policeman for policy'
- Financial disclosures show equity stake in Flashnet, a Bitcoin Lightning payments startup
- Has advisory/investment ties to crypto firms including Bitwise and stablecoin project Basis
- Unlike previous Fed leaders, views bitcoin as monetary policy signal rather than dollar threat
- Known as inflation hawk from previous 2006-2011 Fed tenure
- Potential shift from institutional skepticism to acceptance of bitcoin at highest monetary policy level

# EFF Highlights End-to-End Encryption Advances in Apple-Android RCS Messaging

**TL;DR:** EFF's latest newsletter covers recent progress in encrypted messaging between Apple and Android devices, plus analysis of Canada's surveillance bill and social media restrictions.

## Key Points

- EFF released newsletter covering end-to-end encryption developments for cross-platform messaging
- Apple and Android now support encrypted RCS (Rich Communication Services) messaging
- Newsletter also addresses Canada's surveillance Bill C-22 and social media age verification
- EFF Senior Security Activist Thorin Klosowski discusses encryption progress and setbacks
- Content now available as podcast on major platforms for broader accessibility
- Focus on practical tools for protecting private communications from surveillance
- Educational resource for understanding encryption deployment and policy threats

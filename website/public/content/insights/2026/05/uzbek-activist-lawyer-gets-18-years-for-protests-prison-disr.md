# Uzbek Activist Lawyer Gets 18+ Years for Protests, Prison 'Disruption'

**TL;DR:** Karakalpak lawyer Dauletmurat Tazhimuratov received additional 5-year sentence for 'disrupting prison work,' bringing total to 18.5 years for his role in 2022 protests.

## Key Points

- Dauletmurat Tazhimuratov sentenced to additional 5 years in prison on May 1, 2026
- Combined with previous 16-year sentence for July 2022 Karakalpakstan protest participation
- Latest charges were for 'actions disrupting the work of a penal institution'
- Trial violated defendant rights - no chosen lawyer, family not informed of proceedings
- UN Working Group on Arbitrary Detention called for immediate release in November 2024
- Now classified as 'especially dangerous repeat offender' facing harsher prison conditions
- Allegations of torture and mistreatment in prison have gone unaddressed
- Original 2022 protests saw 21 killed by security forces with no accountability

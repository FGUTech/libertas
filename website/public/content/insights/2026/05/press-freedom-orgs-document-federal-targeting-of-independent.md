# Press Freedom Orgs Document Federal Targeting of Independent Journalists Under Trump

**TL;DR:** CPJ and Amnesty International highlight arrests of multiple independent journalists covering immigration enforcement protests, warning of systematic targeting by federal government.

## Key Points

- Committee to Protect Journalists and Amnesty International USA issued joint warning on World Press Freedom Day
- Independent journalist Georgia Fort arrested in January while covering immigration enforcement protest at Minnesota church
- Additional journalists Don Lemon, Michael Walker Beute, and Junn Bollmann also arrested covering same protest and face federal charges
- Organizations characterize arrests as part of 'larger pattern of targeting journalists by the federal government'
- CPJ draws parallels to repressive government tactics documented internationally
- Press freedom advocates warn continued targeting will lead to unreported injustices
- Cases involve coverage of immigration enforcement, a politically sensitive topic

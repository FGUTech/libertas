# Maldives Government Raids Independent News Outlet, Seizes Digital Equipment

**TL;DR:** Maldives police raided Adhadhu News office, seizing laptops and hard drives over documentary alleging presidential misconduct, while imposing travel bans on journalists.

## Key Points

- Police raided Adhadhu News office in Malé on April 27, seizing journalists' laptops and hard drives
- Raid connected to documentary 'Aisha' that allegedly documented presidential misconduct
- Travel bans imposed on Adhadhu CEO Hussain Fiyaz Moosa and Managing Editor Hassan Mohamed
- Authorities summoned senior staff for questioning following equipment seizure
- Media regulator ordered Channel 13 to halt live broadcast of opposition protests
- Committee to Protect Journalists and 6 other organizations called for return of seized materials
- Case demonstrates real-world threats journalists face: equipment seizure, travel restrictions, censorship

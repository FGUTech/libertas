# EFF Documents Censorship and Digital Isolation of Palestinians in UN Submission

**TL;DR:** EFF submitted evidence to UN Special Rapporteur documenting increased censorship, surveillance, and attacks on Palestinian media infrastructure since October 2023.

## Key Points

- EFF submitted formal evidence to UN Special Rapporteur investigating Palestinian media attacks
- Documents significant deterioration of press freedom since October 2023
- Identifies three key areas: government takedown requests, content moderation issues, and infrastructure attacks
- Reports wave of journalist killings alongside increased digital censorship
- Highlights systematic digital isolation of Palestinian people
- Part of broader UN study on media role in Israel-Palestine context
- Adds to existing documentation of pervasive surveillance regime for Palestinians

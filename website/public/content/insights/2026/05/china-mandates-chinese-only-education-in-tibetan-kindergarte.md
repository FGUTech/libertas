# China Mandates Chinese-Only Education in Tibetan Kindergartens to Erase Native Language

**TL;DR:** China's 2021 'Harmonization Plan' mandates Mandarin-only instruction in Tibetan preschools, with surveillance of home language use, effectively erasing Tibetan language and culture starting at age 3-5.

## Key Points

- 2021 Ministry of Education directive mandates standard Mandarin Chinese for all preschool instruction in ethnic minority areas
- Policy targets kindergarten-age children (3-5 years) during critical language acquisition period
- Parents required to submit videos proving Chinese language use at home under government monitoring
- Many Tibetan children emerge from preschool unable or unwilling to speak Tibetan, even with family
- Curriculum includes mandatory 'patriotic education' and Chinese Communist Party loyalty training for preschoolers
- 2026 Law on Promoting Ethnic Unity imposes legal penalties for 'obstructing' Chinese language learning
- Preschool attendance effectively mandatory despite not being legally required
- Represents systematic information control and cultural surveillance targeting children's identity formation

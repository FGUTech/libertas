# Trump Admin Counterterrorism Strategy Targets Anti-Fascist Activists, Domestic Dissent

**TL;DR:** New 16-page Trump counterterrorism strategy explicitly targets anti-fascist activists alongside ISIS, with 'find you and kill you' language, expanding surveillance powers against domestic dissent.

## Key Points

- Trump administration released new counterterrorism strategy targeting anti-fascist activists, ISIS, and 'narco-terrorists'
- Document contains explicit threat: 'We will find you, and we will kill you' [Source]
- Strategy combines foreign interventions with domestic 'war on dissent' that has proven lethal [Source]
- Administration conducting boat strikes in Caribbean/Pacific that killed nearly 200 people as of early May [Source]
- Legal justification for strikes remains secret, described as 'fake war' where targets don't know they're in conflict [Source]
- Policy built on 25 years of executive overreach and targeted killings via drone warfare [Source]
- Expands post-9/11 legal framework from Afghanistan/Pakistan to Caribbean/Pacific operations
- Intercept reporters describe document as 'list of administration's enemies and promise of what they'll do to them' [Source]

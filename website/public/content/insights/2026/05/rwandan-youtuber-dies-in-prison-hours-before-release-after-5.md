# Rwandan YouTuber Dies in Prison Hours Before Release After 5-Year Sentence

**TL;DR:** Aimable Karasira Uzaramba, a Rwandan YouTube commentator who criticized the government, died of alleged overdose in state custody on his scheduled release day after 5 years imprisonment.

## Key Points

- Aimable Karasira Uzaramba died May 8, 2026 at Nyarugenge hospital on his scheduled prison release day
- Rwanda Correctional Service claims 48-year-old died from overdosing on prescribed medication
- Karasira served 5-year sentence for 'inciting divisions' related to YouTube commentary criticizing government
- Operated YouTube channel 'Ukuri Mbona' (The Truth as I See it) covering genocide narratives and government criticism
- Previously alleged torture, assault, and denial of medical treatment during detention
- CPJ calls for independent investigation given suspicious timing and past torture allegations
- Rwanda ranks as second-worst jailer of journalists in sub-Saharan Africa with 5 currently imprisoned
- Pattern echoes 2020 death of gospel musician Kizito Mihigo in police custody, also ruled suicide

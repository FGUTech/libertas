# Proton Mail Rolls Out Post-Quantum Encryption to All Users

**TL;DR:** Proton Mail now offers post-quantum cryptography protection against future quantum computer attacks, available free to all users with OpenPGP v6 support.

## Key Points

- Proton Mail launched post-quantum encryption protection for all users including free accounts
- Uses post-quantum-ready keys to protect against future quantum computer attacks on RSA/ECC
- Addresses 'harvest now, decrypt later' attacks where encrypted data is collected today for future decryption
- Implements OpenPGP v6 framework to enable modern algorithm support
- Protection applies to new encrypted emails going forward, not retroactively
- Working with Thunderbird and other providers to standardize quantum-safe email across ecosystem
- Gradual rollout currently in progress across user accounts

# Meta Removes Instagram's End-to-End Encryption Feature, Cites Low Adoption

**TL;DR:** Instagram ended its opt-in end-to-end encryption feature, breaking Meta's promise to encrypt DMs by default and eliminating privacy protections on a major platform.

## Key Points

- Instagram discontinued its end-to-end encryption feature for direct messages [EFF]
- Meta broke 2022-2023 public promises to implement E2EE by default across platforms
- Company blamed low adoption rates, ignoring that feature required 4-step opt-in process
- Meta directs users to WhatsApp for encrypted messaging instead of fixing Instagram
- Removal comes as competitors like Google/Apple advance E2EE implementation in RCS
- Facebook Messenger group chat encryption promises also remain undelivered
- Demonstrates how opt-in privacy features fail without default implementation

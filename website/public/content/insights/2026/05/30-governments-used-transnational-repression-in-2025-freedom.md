# 30 Governments Used Transnational Repression in 2025, Freedom House Reports

**TL;DR:** Freedom House documented 126 new incidents of transnational repression in 2025, with 30 governments targeting critics abroad through detention, deportation, and violence.

## Key Points

- 126 new incidents of physical transnational repression recorded in 2025 by Freedom House
- 30 governments reached beyond borders to assassinate, assault, kidnap, detain, and threaten critics
- China, Vietnam, and Russia were top perpetrators; 6 new countries identified as using these tactics
- Over half of incidents (69 of 126) occurred in Southeast Asia and East Africa through authoritarian collaboration
- Detention (49 incidents) and unlawful deportation (48 incidents) were most common tactics
- At least 11 cases involved abuse of Interpol notices against exiled dissidents
- Total of 1,375 incidents documented between 2014-2025 across 107 host countries
- Authoritarian governments increasingly coordinate to circumvent democratic protections for dissidents

# EFF Highlights How Commercial Tracking Feeds Government Mass Surveillance

**TL;DR:** EFF details how widespread commercial tracking enables government surveillance, with data brokers selling personal info to FBI/CBP/ICE to bypass warrant requirements.

## Key Points

- Commercial online tracking enables government surveillance by feeding data broker ecosystem
- Data brokers sell harvested personal data to law enforcement agencies including FBI, CBP, and ICE
- Government exploits this system to buy sensitive information like location data without warrants
- EFF's Privacy Badger browser extension blocks hidden trackers at the source
- EFF advocating for stronger privacy laws and consumer rights in courts
- Weak privacy laws allow this surveillance-industrial complex to operate legally
- Mass surveillance used to control and intimidate people, according to EFF

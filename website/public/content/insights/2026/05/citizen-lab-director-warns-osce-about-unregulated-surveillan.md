# Citizen Lab Director Warns OSCE About Unregulated Surveillance Marketplace

**TL;DR:** Ron Deibert told OSCE that mercenary spyware is just the tip of a massive, unregulated surveillance economy targeting civil society.

## Key Points

- Citizen Lab director Ron Deibert addressed OSCE meeting on digital civil space protection
- Warned that spyware is 'sharp point of the spear' in broader surveillance marketplace
- Called out personal data surveillance economy as foundation for authoritarian tools
- Criticized governments using national security excuses to avoid accountability
- Urged liberal democracies to take concrete action protecting civil society
- Emphasized need to protect those vulnerable to spreading authoritarianism
- Speech delivered at official OSCE human rights forum, indicating institutional recognition of threat

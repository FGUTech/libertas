# Rights Groups Demand Microsoft Disclose Israeli Military Surveillance Tech Review

**TL;DR:** Digital rights coalition demands Microsoft publish findings of its review into Israeli military's use of Azure cloud and AI services for surveillance and targeting of Palestinians.

## Key Points

- Access Now, EFF, Amnesty International and others demand Microsoft publish completed legal review findings
- Microsoft Azure and AI services reportedly used by Israeli military for mass surveillance of Palestinians
- Services allegedly processed intercepted Palestinian communications for lethal targeting operations in Gaza
- Microsoft transferred Israeli branch management to Microsoft France amid scrutiny over defense contracts
- Company announced restrictions on Unit 8200 (Israeli military intelligence) but hasn't disclosed specifics
- Rights groups warn of potential civil and criminal liability for complicity in international law violations
- Organizations demand suspension of services contributing to human rights abuses

# Major Bitcoin Mining Pools Join Stratum v2 Working Group for Mining Decentralization

**TL;DR:** Seven major Bitcoin mining entities including ANTPOOL, Block Inc, and F2Pool joined the Stratum v2 Working Group to advance adoption of the protocol that improves mining decentralization and security.

## Key Points

- ANTPOOL, Block Inc, F2Pool, Foundry, Spiderpool, MARA Foundation, and DMND joined Stratum v2 Working Group
- Stratum v2 protocol enables individual miners to construct their own block templates, reducing pool centralization
- Protocol provides end-to-end encryption and improved efficiency for mining operations
- Working group founded in 2022 by Braiins and Spiral to develop open, vendor-neutral specification
- New members represent substantial mining ecosystem players, indicating industry consensus on protocol direction
- Protocol supports better management of large miner fleets and improves bandwidth efficiency
- Adoption could strengthen Bitcoin's resistance to mining centralization and censorship

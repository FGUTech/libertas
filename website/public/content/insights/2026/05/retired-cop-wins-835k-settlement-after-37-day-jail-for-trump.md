# Retired Cop Wins $835K Settlement After 37-Day Jail for Trump Meme

**TL;DR:** Larry Bushart won $835,000 settlement after Tennessee sheriff jailed him 37 days for posting Trump meme on Facebook, establishing precedent for First Amendment violations.

## Key Points

- Larry Bushart, retired Tennessee police officer, jailed 37 days for Facebook Trump meme post
- Bushart sued county and sheriff claiming censorship and First Amendment violation
- Settlement reached for $835,000 with lawsuit dismissal agreement
- Foundation for Individual Rights and Expression (FIRE) represented Bushart
- Case demonstrates legal remedies available for government speech suppression
- Substantial financial penalty signals courts take social media censorship seriously
- Precedent established for similar First Amendment violation cases

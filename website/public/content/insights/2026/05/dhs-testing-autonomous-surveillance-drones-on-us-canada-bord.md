# DHS Testing Autonomous Surveillance Drones on US-Canada Border

**TL;DR:** DHS will deploy autonomous drones and ground vehicles streaming battlefield intelligence over 5G networks along the US-Canada border in a bilateral experiment this fall.

## Key Points

- Department of Homeland Security conducting autonomous drone surveillance experiment
- Test involves both aerial drones and ground-based autonomous vehicles
- Systems will stream 'battlefield intelligence' over 5G networks
- Operation targets US-Canada border region
- Bilateral experiment suggests Canadian government participation
- Deployment scheduled for fall 2024
- Represents escalation in automated border surveillance capabilities
- Could establish precedent for expanded autonomous surveillance programs

# South Carolina Bans CBDC Payments, Supports Crypto Mining

**TL;DR:** South Carolina governor signed legislation banning state entities from accepting CBDCs and providing strong support for cryptocurrency mining operations.

## Key Points

- South Carolina governor signed pro-cryptocurrency legislation into law
- New law prohibits state entities from accepting or requiring CBDC payments
- Legislation includes provisions supporting cryptocurrency mining operations
- Represents state-level resistance to federal digital currency initiatives
- Adds South Carolina to growing list of states rejecting CBDCs
- Mining support could attract Bitcoin infrastructure investment to the state
- Policy creates regulatory clarity for crypto businesses in South Carolina

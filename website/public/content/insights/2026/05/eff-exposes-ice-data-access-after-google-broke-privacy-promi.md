# EFF Exposes ICE Data Access After Google Broke Privacy Promise to Client

**TL;DR:** EFF reveals case where Google handed client data to ICE despite privacy promises, highlighting corporate failure to protect users from government surveillance overreach.

## Key Points

- EFF client's data was given to ICE after Google broke its privacy promise [EFF]
- Case featured in EFFector newsletter covering surveillance and corporate accountability
- EFF Senior Staff Attorney F. Mario Trujillo discusses state attorneys general role in holding Google accountable
- Newsletter also covers ongoing NSA Section 702 surveillance reform efforts
- Highlights broader pattern of tech companies failing to protect users from government data requests
- Demonstrates gap between corporate privacy promises and actual protection during government pressure
- EFF pushing for stronger corporate accountability mechanisms through state-level enforcement

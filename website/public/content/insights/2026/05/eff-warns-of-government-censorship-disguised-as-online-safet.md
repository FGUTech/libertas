# EFF Warns of Government Censorship Disguised as Online Safety Measures

**TL;DR:** EFF's latest analysis reveals how governments are using 'child safety' as pretext to implement dangerous censorship systems, including social media bans and VPN restrictions.

## Key Points

- EFF published analysis warning against government censorship systems disguised as online safety measures
- California proposing social media ban that could set dangerous precedent for online censorship
- Utah implementing new law regulating VPN usage, attacking core privacy tool
- Age verification gates being promoted as solution but may create broader control mechanisms
- EFF Legislative Analyst Molly Buckley discusses constitutional challenges to social media bans
- Pattern of governments using child safety rhetoric to justify restrictions on digital rights
- EFF frames these as attacks on fundamental internet freedom infrastructure

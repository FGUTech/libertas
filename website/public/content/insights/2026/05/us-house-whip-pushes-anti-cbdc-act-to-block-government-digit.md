# US House Whip Pushes Anti-CBDC Act to Block Government Digital Currency

**TL;DR:** House Majority Whip Tom Emmer is advocating for his Anti-CBDC Surveillance State Act, which passed the House but awaits Senate action to permanently ban central bank digital currencies.

## Key Points

- Tom Emmer (House Majority Whip) pushing Anti-CBDC Surveillance State Act
- Bill has passed House but still needs Senate approval
- Legislation aims to permanently ban central bank digital currencies (CBDCs)
- Bill title explicitly frames CBDCs as surveillance infrastructure
- Part of broader congressional debate over housing bill provisions
- Represents legislative resistance to government-controlled digital money
- Senate passage remains uncertain for permanent CBDC prohibition

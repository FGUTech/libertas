# White House to Announce Strategic Bitcoin Reserve Details After Legal Framework Completion

**TL;DR:** U.S. officials say announcement on Strategic Bitcoin Reserve implementation is imminent after clearing legal hurdles for custody of 328,372 BTC held from law enforcement seizures.

## Key Points

- Patrick Witt (Executive Director, President's Council of Advisors for Digital Assets) says SBR announcement coming soon
- Administration has completed legal framework for custody and reporting infrastructure across federal agencies
- Reserve currently holds 328,372 BTC (~1.6% of global supply) from law enforcement seizures including Silk Road
- Executive order from March 6, 2025 bars Treasury from selling any Bitcoin holdings
- $70M+ in cryptocurrency thefts from U.S. Marshals Service highlighted need for proper custody solutions
- Two Congressional bills (BITCOIN Act/ARMA and Lummis bill) would codify reserve into law
- Proposed legislation would authorize Treasury to purchase 200,000 BTC annually for five years
- If passed, U.S. would become first nation to actively accumulate Bitcoin as strategic reserve asset
